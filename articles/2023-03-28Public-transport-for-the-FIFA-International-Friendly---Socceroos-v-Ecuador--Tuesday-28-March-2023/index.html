Public transport for the FIFA International Friendly – Socceroos v Ecuador: Tuesday 28 March 2023

International Soccer is back as the Socceroos return to Marvel Stadium to take on Ecuador, and public transport is your best way to enjoy it. Extra services will help you get home from Marvel Stadium on Tuesday 28 March.

Kick-off is at 7:30pm.
