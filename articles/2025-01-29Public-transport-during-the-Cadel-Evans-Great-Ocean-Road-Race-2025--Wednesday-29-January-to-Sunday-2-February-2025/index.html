Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Make your journey hassle-free and remember to top up your myki and have it ready to use before travelling.

Please refer to the Cadel Evans Great Ocean Road Race website for further event information, road closures and accessibility information.

Planned closures

Due to the Cadel Evans Great Ocean Road Race in Geelong, the Bellarine and Surf Coast bus Routes 1, 20, 24, 30, 31, 41, 42, 43, 50, 51, 52, 53, 54, 55, 56, 60, 61 and 101 (Apollo Bay to Geelong) will experience delays, be diverted in both directions and selected bus stops will not be serviced on Wednesday 29 January, Thursday 30 January, Saturday 1 and Sunday 2 February 2025.

Additionally, on Saturday 1 February, the Warrnambool Line will be suspended between Waurn Ponds and Warrnambool, affecting the 1:10pm Southern Cross to Warrnambool and 5:36pm Warrnambool to Melbourne services. Replacement coaches will be available from Geelong Station.

Passengers can catch regular services arriving at Geelong and Marshall Railway Station and connect with bus services to Torquay.

Leaving Geelong

Passengers can return via bus services from Torquay and catch regular services departing Geelong and Marshall Railway Station.
