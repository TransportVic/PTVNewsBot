Routes 216, 219 and 220 changing around St Kilda Road

Due to works on St Kilda Road and Southbank Boulevard, routes 216, 219 and 220 no longer run along St Kilda Road between the city and Commercial Road. 

Since Sunday 9 September 2018, buses from Gardenvale terminate at the Alfred Hospital and buses from Sunshine terminate on Queen Street, near Collins Street in the city.

To continue your journey along St Kilda Road connect with tram routes 3, 3a, 5, 6, 16, 64, 67 or 72 on Swanston Street or St Kilda Road.

Plan ahead and find your travel options using our Journey Planner.
