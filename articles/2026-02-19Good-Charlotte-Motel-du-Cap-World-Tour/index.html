Good Charlotte Motel du Cap World Tour

Extra services to and from Bendigo Jockey Club (Bendigo Racecourse) on Saturday 21 February.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey. 

If you use Journey Planner, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Regional train

Getting to Bendigo

Passengers can catch regular services to Bendigo Railway Station and then connect with free bus shuttle services operating to and from Bendigo Jockey Club. 

Leaving Bendigo 

Passengers can board free bus shuttle services from Bendigo Jockey Club and connect to trains departing Bendigo Railway Station.

To assist patrons leaving the event, VLine will run additional train services from Bendigo to Melbourne (Southern Cross) departing:

  • Bendigo to Melbourne departing 10.50pm
  • Bendigo to Melbourne departing 11.32pm

Regional bus

Getting to Bendigo Jockey Club

Passengers can catch a free bus shuttle running between Bendigo Station and the Bendigo Jockey Club from 3.30pm to 6.30pm. Bus shuttle services will align with train arrivals and departures.

Leaving Bendigo Jockey Club

Passengers can catch a free bus shuttle running on a continuous loop after the event, stopping at:

  • Bendigo Visitor Centre
  • Bendigo Railway Station

For further information regarding the shuttles, please visit the event website.
