Public transport for The Weeknd in Concert

The Weeknd has arrived in Melbourne, set to take to the stage at Marvel Stadium! We're running extra services to and from Marvel Stadium on Saturday 5 and Sunday 6 October. 

Doors open at 5pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Saturday 5 October

Extra trains run to get you to and from the concert.

 Metropolitan train

Getting to Marvel Stadium via Southern Cross 

Arriving at Flinders Street? You can either connect with trains to Southern Cross or stay onboard if continuing there.

  • Heidelberg: Leaves at 3:28pm, arriving at Flinders Street 3:54pm (Platform 2) and then Southern Cross at 4pm. 
  • Mernda: Leaves at 3:10pm, arriving at Flinders Street at 4:02pm (Platform 1) and then Southern Cross at 4:07pm. 
  • Glen Waverley: Leaves at 3:29pm, arriving at Flinders Street 4:04pm (Platform 4) and then Southern Cross at 4:10pm. 
  • Sandringham: Leaves at 3:45pm, arriving at Flinders Street at 4:15pm (Platform 12) and then Southern Cross at 4:26pm. 
  • Craigieburn: Leaves at 3:08pm and 3:28pm. 

Leaving Marvel Stadium via Southern Cross 

Leaving from Southern Cross? Take a direct train or connect to trains heading to Flinders Street.

  • Heidelberg leaving at 11:10pm, 11:31pm and 11:39pm from Platform 9. 
  • Mernda leaving at 11:14pm, 11:24pm and 11:44pm from Platform 9. 
  • Glen Waverley leaving at 11:12pm, 11:22pm and 11:45pm from Platform 10. 
  • Ringwood leaving at 11:18pm, 11:28pm and 11:51pm from Platform 10. 
  • Werribee leaving at 11:25pm and 11:43pm from Platform 14. 
  • Upfield leaving at 11:29pm from Platform 11. 
  • Craigieburn leaving at 11:13pm and 11:34pm from Platform 11. 
  • Frankston leaving at 11:16pm and 11:42pm from Platform 13, and then Flinders Street at 11:25pm and 11:48pm from Platform 7. 
  • Cheltenham at 11:25pm and 11:37pm from Platform 12, and then Flinders Street at 11:31pm and 11:43pm from Platform 7. 
  • Sandringham at 11:25pm and 11:21pm from Platform 13, and then Flinders Street at 11:11pm and 11:27pm from Platform 12.  

 Metropolitan tram

Extra trams run before and after the concert on Route 30 and Route 86 to and from Stop 1 Bourke Street/ Spencer Street and Stop D1-Docklands Stadium/La Trobe St (Docklands). 

 Regional train

Normal services will operate to and from Southern Cross before and after the concert.
