Dogs and Cats Lovers Festival

The Dogs & Cats Lovers Festival will return to Melbourne at the Melbourne Showgrounds! Public transport is your best way to enjoy it, with extra services to help you get to and from the Melbourne Showgrounds on Saturday 25 and Sunday 26 October.

The event will run 9.30am to 5pm each day.

If you use Journey Planner within 14 days of this event, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.
