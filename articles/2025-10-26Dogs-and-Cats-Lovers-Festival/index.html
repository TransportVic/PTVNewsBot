Dogs and Cats Lovers Festival

The Dogs & Cats Lovers Festival will return to Melbourne at the Melbourne Showgrounds! Public transport is your best way to enjoy it, with extra services to help you get to and from the Melbourne Showgrounds on Saturday 25 and Sunday 26 October.

The event will run 9.30am to 5pm each day.

Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner within 14 days of this event, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Metropolitan train

Getting to Melbourne Showgrounds

Trains will operate from Southern Cross (Platform 8) and North Melbourne (Platforms 1/2) stopping in both directions, then express to the Showgrounds (Platform 1).

The last train is to depart from the Showgrounds at approximately 5.50pm.

Metropolitan tram

Regular and extra Trams will operate via Route 57 , with extra trams operating via Route 57a between Stop 01 Flinders Street / Elizabeth Street and Stop 32 Sandown Road/ Epsom Road via Flemington Road, to and from the Showgrounds. 

Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Melbourne Showgrounds

The following bus route travels close to Flemington Racecourse: 

  • Route 472 (Williamstown to Moonee Ponds via Footscray) 

Regional train

Getting to Melbourne Showgrounds

Passengers arriving at North Melbourne and Southern Cross stations are advised to change trains and catch a Melbourne Showgrounds service. 

Refer to the Metropolitan trains section above for service times and platform information. 

Leaving the Melbourne Showgrounds

Passengers are advised to catch any train from Melbourne Showgrounds and alight at Southern Cross or North Melbourne stations and change to a regional train. 

Refer to the Metropolitan train section above for service times.
