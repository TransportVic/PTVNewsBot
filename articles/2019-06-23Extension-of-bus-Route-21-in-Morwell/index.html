Extension of bus Route 21 in Morwell

From 23 June 2019, the existing Route 21 Morwell to Mid Valley Shopping Centre via Crinigan Road will be extended to the new GovHub currently under construction. Two new stops are being added to provide access to GovHub, as well as the community health centre and Lifeline.

When travelling to Morwell Station, Route 21 will no longer turn out of Church Street into New Street. Instead it will continue along Church Street and turn right into Buckley Street, right into Collins Street and left into Mary Street before continuing along the current route path.

Coming from Morwell Station, the route will no longer turn left into Mary Street on to Collins Street. It will turn right into Collins Street, left on to Buckley Street, left into Church Street and then continue on its current route path.

The new timetable is available online and in Journey Planner. To order a printed copy, call 1800 800 007.
