Route 25 will connect with shops at Delacombe Town Centre

From Sunday 22 November 2020, bus Route 25 will be extended to Delacombe town centre to give you a more convenient trip to the shops. There will also be a minor change to the timetable to allow for the route adjustment.  

Departing Delacombe to Ballarat Station: Route 25 will run from 5.40am to 7.45pm on weekdays, 7.30am to 5.20pm Saturdays and 8.30am to 4.30pm Sundays.
