Timetable change for Route 45 in Armstrong Creek

From Monday 25 January 2021, bus Route 45 Armstrong Creek – Waurn Ponds Shopping Centre via Waurn Ponds Station will have a minor timetable change.

One afternoon weekday trip will be brought forward by 17 minutes to better coordinate with bell times at Oberon High School’s new campus in Armstrong Creek. Route 45 will depart from Waralily Shopping Centre at 3.41pm, reducing the wait time for students catching Route 45 to get home. 

View the new Route 45 timetable.

Planning is underway to ensure students will have continued access to public transport as the school relocates.

To plan your journey, visit Journey planner. To order a printed copy of the timetable, call 1800 800 007.
