Public transport for the World Supercross Australian Grand Prix: Friday 21 October and Saturday 22 October 2022

World Supercross Grand Prix is returning to Melbourne. Extra services will help you get home from Marvel Stadium.

The event will begin at 6pm.

Plan your trip to the cup using Journey planner.

Before you travel

 

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Getting to and from the cup

 Metropolitan train

  • Mernda departing at 10:16pm, Platform 2
  • Eltham departing at 10:11pm, Platform 2

Trains depart Southern Cross after the event ends, as follows:

  • Lilydale departing at 9:49pm, Platform 10
  • Glen Waverley departing at 9.45pm, Platform 10
  • Pakenham departing at 9:45pm, Platform 12
  • Frankston departing at 9:55pm, Platform 13
  • Sandringham departing 9:44pm, Platform 13
  • Werribee departing at 9:43pm, Platform 14
  • Craigieburn departing at 9:47pm, Platform 14
  • Sunbury departing at 9:55pm, Platform 14

Plan your journey using Journey Planner. 

 Regional train 

Normal timetable trains and coaches will operate. 

 Metropolitan tram 

Getting to and from Marvel Stadium 

Trams will operate as Route 30  between East Melbourne and Stop D1 Marvel Stadium via La Trobe Street, and as Route 86 between East Melbourne and Stop D1 Marvel Stadium via Bourke Street. 

Plan your journey using Journey Planner. 

 Metropolitan bus 

Getting to and from Marvel Stadium 

Passengers can catch the following bus routes close to Marvel Stadium: 

  • Route 232 (Altona North to City)
  • Route 235 (Fishermans Bend to City)
  • Route 302 (Box Hill to City)
  • Route 304 (Doncaster SC to City)
  • Route 905 (The Pines SC to City)
  • Route 906 (Warrandyte to City)
  • Route 907 (Mitcham to City)

Please note bus services may finish prior to the end of the event
