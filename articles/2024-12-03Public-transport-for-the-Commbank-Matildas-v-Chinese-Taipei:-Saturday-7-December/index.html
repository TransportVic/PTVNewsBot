Public transport for the Commbank Matildas v Chinese Taipei: Saturday 7 December

The Commbank Matildas are coming to GMHBA Stadium to take on Chinese Taipei. This matchup is sure to deliver edge-of-your-seat action and Public transport is your best way to enjoy it! Extra services will help you get home from GMHBA Stadium on Saturday 7 December 2024.

  • Gates open: 5:45pm
  • Event starts: 7:30pm

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Please note, due to road closures implemented on Moorabool Street for pedestrian safety before and after the game, service changes will apply from 6:15pm until last services. Please see further information below for more information.

Getting to GMHBA

Normal services will operate to South Geelong Station to get you to GMHBA Stadium.

Getting home from GMHBA

Normal and additional services will operate after the game from South Geelong Station.

An additional service will depart South Geelong Station to Southern Cross Station at 9:50pm.

Regional bus

Customers can catch the following buses running close to GMHBA Stadium:

  • Route 01
  • Route 24
  • Route 42
  • Route 53
  • Route 55
  • Route 101 (Apollo Bay-Geelong)

Passengers please note, bus services may conclude prior to the end of the game.

Due to road closures implemented on Moorabool Street for pedestrian safety, service changes will apply from 6:15pm until last services. Due to this there will be changes to the following Bus Routes 1, 24, 42, 53, 55 and 101 which will not service selected bus stops on Moorabool, Lonsdale Street and instead, travel via Yarra Street and South Geelong Station.

Buses will not service the following bus stops during road closures:

  • Moorabool Street/Balliang Street (Stop ID 30676)
  • Moorabool Street/Park Crescent (Stop ID 30744)
  • Moorabool Street/Kardinia Park and Ride (Stop ID 30745)
  • Kardinia Park and Ride/Moorabool Street (Stop ID 30675)
  • Moorabool Street/Kardinia Park (Stop ID 30746)
  • Moorabool Street/Lonsdale Street (Stop ID 47749)
  • Lonsdale Street/South Geelong Station (Stop ID 45073)

Passengers are advised to use existing bus stops at:

  • Moorabool Street/Kilgour Street (Stop ID 30673)
  • Moorabool Street/Maud Street (Stop ID 47584)

Passengers can also use temporary bus stops at:

  • South Geelong Railway Station/Lonsdale Street (26 Lonsdale Street; in Yarra Street opposite Stop ID 41423)
  • Yarra Street/Balliang Street (262 Yarra Street, 259 Yarra Street)
