Apple Wallet Payment Issue

Fares and tickets > myki Pass

Topping up via Apple Pay at a myki machine, quick top up machine or ticket office is currently unavailable.​ We are working with Apple to fix this issue as soon as possible.​

In the meantime, you can still top up online via the PTV app, or by using a physical credit or bank card at:​

  • hundreds of shops including 7-Eleven within the myki boundary​
  • myki machines at selected stations and stops​
  • premium station ticket offices​
