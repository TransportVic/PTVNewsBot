More services for Route 512 Strathmore – East Coburg

From Sunday 28 February 2021, the deviation around Coonans Hill, Pascoe South will be removed from the Route 512 Strathmore – East Coburg via Coburg, Coburg West & Pascoe Vale South bus route.

The change allows for a more consistent and reliable timetable with an extra 64 new services, arriving on average every 20 minutes on weekdays and on average every 40 minutes on Saturdays.

Passengers that use existing stops at Coonans Hill may use the nearby bus stop on Reynard Street for Route 512, the bus stop on Moreland Road to access Route 510, or access trams from the tram stop on Melville Road.

View the Route 510 and new Route 512 timetables.
