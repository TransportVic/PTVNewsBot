Before you travel

Please continue to check this page for more details and up-to-date information before you travel.

When travelling on public transport you must wear a fitted face mask covering your nose and mouth, unless you have a lawful reason for not doing so, and ensure you wear it for your entire journey.

Getting to and from Bendigo

 Regional train (V/Line)

To Bendigo

Normal timetable services will operate from Southern Cross Station.

The last train from Southern Cross Station to Bendigo departs will depart at 11.55pm.

From Bendigo

Normal timetable services will operate from Bendigo Station.

The last train from Bendigo to Southern Cross Station will depart at 9.40pm.

Additional coaches will depart every hour from 11:00pm to 2:00am, see below for detail.

 Regional coach (V/Line)

To Melbourne

Additional coaches will run from Bendigo to Melbourne from 11:00pm to 2:00am on an hourly frequency.

All V/Line coaches will arrive and depart from Bendigo Station.

 Regional bus

Buses in Bendigo will run to their normal timetable until 8pm. From 4pm road closures will be in the place and the below services will be affected.

Routes 5, 50, 51, 52, 53, 54, 55, 60, 61, 65 (Bendigo): Temporary bus stop closures for White Night

From 6pm to 1:30am, five special White Night bus routes will operate every 15 minutes, to and from the Bendigo town centre.

These routes run to and from the bus hub in Williamson Street.

No buses will pick up or drop off from Bendigo Station.

White Night Hybrid route bus stops.

 Park and Ride

From 6pm until 1:30am, there will be four Park & Ride locations in Bendigo. You can park your car and catch an express bus to the Williamson St bus hub from:

  • Bendigo Racecourse - Heinz St, Ascot
  • Lansell Square - High St, Kangaroo Flat
  • La Trobe University - Edwards Rd, Flora Hill
  • Bendigo Stadium - Inglis St, West Bendigo

Bendigo City Tram

Trams will be operating from the Central Deborah Gold Mine on Violet Street and Bendigo Joss House on Finn Street to the White Night precinct every 20 minutes from 6pm to 2am.

Bendigo Tramways operate high floor trams with steps, so they are unsuitable for mobility aids including wheelchairs, scooters and motorised vehicles.

This is not a myki service. Fare is $5 per person
