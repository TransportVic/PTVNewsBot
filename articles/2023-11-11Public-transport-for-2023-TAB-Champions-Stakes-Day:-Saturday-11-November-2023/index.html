Public transport for 2023 TAB Champions Stakes Day: Saturday 11 November 2023

With Stakes Day on Saturday 11 November, it's the last hurrah for the 2023 Spring Racing Carnival and time for some family fun at Flemington Racecourse!

There will be additional services so you can get to and from the Races easily.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Make your journey hassle-free and remember to top up your myki and have it ready to use before travelling.

Stakes Day - Saturday 11 November 2023

All services will operate to a normal Saturday timetable, with additional services as noted below.

  • Gates open: 10.30am
  • First Race: 12.40pm
  • Feature Race: 5.10pm
  • Last Race: 5.50pm

 Metropolitan train

Getting to the Racecourse

Flinders Street Station services will depart from Platform 8/9 and run express to Flemington Racecourse:

  • From 10.12am to 1.23pm, every 6 to 7 minutes
  • From 1.35pm to 4.52pm, every 10 minutes (services will also stop at Southern Cross Platform 14 and North Melbourne Platform 6 stations)

Southern Cross Station services will depart from Platform 8 stopping at North Melbourne Station (Platform 2) and then run express to Flemington Racecourse:

  • From 10.25am to 1.33pm, every 10 minutes

Leaving the Racecourse

Services will depart Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1/2) and run express to North Melbourne (Platform 5), Southern Cross (Platform 13) and Flinders Street (Platform 8/9) stations only:

  • From 1.27pm to 5.15pm, every 10 minutes
  • From 5.23pm to 7.36pm, every 4 to 5 minutes
  • From 7:46pm to 8.59pm, every 10 minutes

Please note: The last service to depart Flemington Racecourse will be at 8.59pm.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.

Footscray Station bus shuttle

Passengers for the Sunbury, Werribee and Williamstown lines can catch a one-way shuttle bus from Leonard Crescent (outside Flemington Racecourse Station) express to Footscray Station:

  • From 4.30pm to 8.30pm, every 5 minutes

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from the Racecourse

Additional services will operate on Route 57a from Elizabeth Street to Epsom Road (Melbourne Showgrounds):

These services will operator as a shuttle service and will stop all stops between Stop 1 Flinders St Station and Stop 19 Royal Children’s Hospital, then express to Stop 30 Flemington Racecourse

  • From 10.30am to 1:30pm and 4:00pm to 8:00pm

 Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from the Racecourse

The following bus route travels close to Flemington Racecourse:

  • Route 404 (Footscray - Moonee Ponds via Newmarket)

 Regional train

Getting to the Racecourse

Passengers arriving at Southern Cross Station coach terminal are advised to walk to Platforms 8 or 14 and catch a Flemington Racecourse service.

Refer to the Metropolitan train section above for service times and platform information.

Leaving the Racecourse

Passengers can catch any service departing Flemington Racecourse, alight at Southern Cross Station and then walk to the coach terminal.
