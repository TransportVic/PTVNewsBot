Let's Go with more buses between the city and Fishermans Bend

From Monday 31 October 2022, a turn up and go service will operate on Route 235 City - Fishermans Bend via Williamstown Road and Route 237 City - Fishermans Bend via Lorimer Street.

Buses will run about every 10 minutes across the two routes on weekdays.

More than 900 services a week will be added to the two routes, improving access between Fishermans Bend and the CBD.

The direction of the routes, service operating hours and the frequency of weekend services will remain unchanged.

To plan your journey, view the new timetable for Route 235 and Route 237, visit Journey planner or call 1800 800 007 to order a printed copy of your timetable.
