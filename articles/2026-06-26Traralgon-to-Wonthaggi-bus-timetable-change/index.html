Traralgon to Wonthaggi bus timetable change

From Monday 6 July 2026, the weekday morning Traralgon to Wonthaggi bus will depart 1 hour and 19 minutes earlier.

The bus will now depart Traralgon Station at 9:03am and arrive at Wonthaggi Bus Interchange at 10:58am.

This change will give passengers more time in Wonthaggi before the 1:35pm return trip.
