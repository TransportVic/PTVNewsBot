New Sunday services for Route 612

From Sunday 25 August 2024, Route 612 Box Hill Station – Chadstone via Surrey Hills & Camberwell & Glen Iris will run on Sundays for the first time.

There will be 23 services every Sunday, making it easier to get to places like Chadstone Shopping Centre, Camberwell Market, Camberwell Station, Glen Iris Station, Box Hill Station and Union Station.

The Sunday services will operate every 60 minutes from 7.19 am to 6.37 pm and connect with trains at Glen Iris Station.

The Route 612 weekday and Saturday timetable won’t change. 

To plan your journey, view the Route 612 timetable, download the PTV app, visit Journey planner or call 1800 800 007 to order a printed copy of your timetable.
