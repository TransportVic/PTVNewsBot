We’re adding 470 weekly trips across routes 170, 180, 190 and 192

From 1 July 2025, we’re adding more buses, more often on routes 170, 180, 190 and 192.

Buses will run later most days, giving you more ways to travel at times that work for you.

New timetables mean buses will come every 20 minutes most of the day. 

It’ll be easier to get to work, go shopping or catch the train at Werribee, Tarneit and Wyndham Vale stations.

To plan your journey:

  • Download the PTV app
  • Use the journey planner
  • Check the route 170, 180, 190 and 192 timetables
  • or call 1800 800 007.

What’s changing for each route?

  • 81 new weekly trips.
  • Services will run every 20 minutes on average across the week.
  • Weekday services will run later until 12:29 am, instead of 9:29 pm.
  • Sunday services will run later until 9:59 pm, instead of 9:29 pm.

Route 180 Werribee Station – Tarneit Station via Tarneit Rd

  • 98 new weekly trips.
  • Services will run every 20 minutes on average across the week.
  • Weekday services will run later until 11:59 pm, instead of 9:29 pm.
  • Saturday services will run later until 11:59 pm instead of 11:29 pm.
  • Sunday services will run later until 9:59 pm, instead of 9:29 pm.

Route 190 Wyndham Vale Station – Werribee Station via Ballan Rd

  • 25 new weekly trips.
  • Services will run every 20 minutes on average across the week.
  • Weekday services will run later until 12:59 am, instead of 11:59 pm.

Route 192 Wyndham Vale Station – Werribee Station via Black Forest Rd

  • 266 new weekly trips.
  • Services will run every 20 minutes on average across the week, instead of every 40 minutes.
  • Weekday and Saturday services will run later until 11:59 pm, instead of 9:29 pm.
  • Sunday services will run later until 9:59 pm, instead of 9:29 pm.
