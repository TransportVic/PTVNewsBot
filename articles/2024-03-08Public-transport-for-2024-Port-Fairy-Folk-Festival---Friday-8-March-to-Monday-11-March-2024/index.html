Public transport for 2024 Port Fairy Folk Festival: Friday 8 March to Monday 11 March 2024

Get ready for the Port Fairy Folk Festival in Port Fairy!

There will be additional services so you can get to and from the event easily.

Campbell Street, Port Fairy

Friday 8 March to Monday 11 March, 2024

Event start: 4pm Friday 8 March

Event finish: 2pm Monday 11 March

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Make your journey hassle-free and remember to top up your myki and have it ready to use before travelling.

Please refer to Port Fairy Folk Festival website for further event information, set times and accessibility information.

Passengers can also find further travel information here.

 Regional train

Getting to Port Fairy

Passengers can catch regular services arriving at Warrnambool Station.

An additional coach service will depart Warrnambool to Port Fairy on Friday 8 March at 4:50pm.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.

Leaving Port Fairy

Passengers can catch regular services departing Warrnambool Station.

An additional coach services will depart Port Fairy to Warrnambool on Monday 11 March at 11:38am and 4:37pm.

Service Disruptions

  • Warrnambool to Melbourne via Ararat and Hamilton
  • Mt Gambier to Melbourne via Warrnambool and Geelong

Passengers, please note that due to the road closures implemented for the event, VLine services to and from Port Fairy will operate via a temporary bus stop and will not service the following stops from 4pm Friday 8 to last service Monday 11 March:

  •  Bank Street/Port Fairy Visitor Centre ID 36550

Passengers please board services from the temporary bus stop at:

  • 40 Bank Street, (in James Street opposite the Victoria Hotel)

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.
