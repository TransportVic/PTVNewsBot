Public transport for Altitude: Elements of Unity

Events > Culture

We're running extra services to and from Altitude: Elements of Unity at Flemington Racecourse on Friday 13 September.

Event starts at 4pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

 Metropolitan train

From the Racecourse

Catch a Craigieburn Line train from Flinders Street Station to Newmarket Station.

Passengers can then catch a Route 57 tram and at Stop 29 Racecourse Road/Flemington Racecourse.

To the Racecourse

Trains run from Flemington Racecourse to Flinders Street Station.

Trains leave:

  • Every 30 minutes from 9:06pm to 10:58pm

  • Every 10-15 minutes from 10:58pm to 11:41pm

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Catch a Route 57 tram to Stop 29 Racecourse Road/Flemington Racecourse.

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

 Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Catch a Route 404 bus to and from the Racecourse. The last service may run before the event ends.

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

 Regional train

Getting to Flemington Racecourse

Catch a train from North Melbourne or Southern Cross stations to Flemington Racecourse.

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

From Flemington Racecourse

Catch a train from Flemington Racecourse to North Melbourne or Southern Cross stations for regional trains.
