Public transport for Monster Jam: Saturday 5 October 2024

Events > Sport

Monster Jam returns to AAMI Park and public transport is your best way to enjoy it! We're running extra services to get you to and from AAMI Park on Saturday 5 October.

The event has two sessions: 

  • Session 1 starts at 1pm and finishes at 3pm 
  • Session 2 starts at 7pm and finishes at 9pm 

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

 Metropolitan train

Extra trains run to get you home.

Session 1

Return trains from Richmond Station 

  • Glen Waverley leaving at 3:22pm from Platform 10 
  • Sandringham leaving at 3:14pm from Platform 2 

Session 2 

Return trains from Richmond Station 

  • Glen Waverley leaving at 9:24pm from Platform 10 
  • Ringwood leaving at 9:28pm from Platform 9 
  • Sandringham leaving at 9:14pm from Platform 2 
  • Cheltenham leaving at 9:12pm from Platform 4 
  • Frankston leaving at 9:32pm from Platform 4 

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from AAMI Park

Extra Route 70 trams run between Flinders Street / Elizabeth Street (Stop 4) and Stop 7C MCG/John Cain Arena.

 Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from the MCG:

Catch a Route 246 bus close to the MCG. The last service may run before session 2 ends.

 Regional train

Connect to AAMI Park via Tram Route 70 or 70a from Flinders Street, or take a train to and from Richmond Station.
