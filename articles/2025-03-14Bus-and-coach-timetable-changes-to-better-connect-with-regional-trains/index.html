Bus and coach timetable changes to better connect with regional trains

From 13 April 2025, there will be new bus and coach timetables to better connect with new train timetables on the Ballarat and Warrnambool lines. On weekends, 662 extra one-way bus trips will be added along the Ballarat Line.

Visit April 2025 regional timetable change for more information on the new Ballarat and Warrnambool line timetables.

To plan your journey:

  • Download the PTV app
  • Use the journey planner
  • or call 1800 800 007.

Ouyen to Ballarat coach

  • The current 6:25 am Sunday coach from Ouyen will end at Ararat instead of Ballarat.
  • Passengers wishing to travel through to Ballarat will need to change to a train service at Ararat Station.
  • The 6:25am Sunday coach arrives at Ararat Station at 10:45 am. A train service to Ballarat departs Ararat Station at 10:57 am.
  • Passengers will have 12 minutes to transfer between these two services.
  • This change removes duplication between coach and train services.
