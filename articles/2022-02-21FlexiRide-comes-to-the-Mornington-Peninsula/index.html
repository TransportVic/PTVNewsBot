FlexiRide comes to the Mornington Peninsula

From Monday 21 February 2022, we’re introducing the new on-demand bus service FlexiRide to the Mornington Peninsula and making changes to Route 787 and Route 886. These changes will give residents in Safety Beach, Dromana, McRae and Rosebud a faster and more convenient way to travel.

FlexiRide Rosebud

FlexiRide is a 12-month trial and will replace bus Route 787 between Safety Beach and Rosebud, as well as Route 886.

FlexiRide has no fixed route and only operates when booked. Passengers can book a trip from their nearest physical or virtual bus stop and travel to and from the designated FlexiRide bus hubs within the same service area. FlexiRide Rosebud bus hubs are located at Dromana Hub Shopping Centre, Rosebud Plaza, Rosebud Hospital and Rosebud Village Shopping Centre.

FlexiRide will operate between 8am – 3.45pm on weekdays. Services do not run on weekends or public holidays.

To find your nearest stop location or to book FlexiRide, call (03) 8710 6377 between 5am – 7.30pm or download the FlexiRide app from Google Play or the Apple App Store. You can book up to seven days in advance.  Bookings via the app can be cancelled at any time.

Please let us know if you have accessibility needs when making a booking. Passengers with accessibility needs can register for home pickup by calling (03) 8710 6377.

Route 787 Sorrento to Rosebud

Route 787 will operate between Sorrento and Rosebud. On weekdays, the upgraded Route 787 will run hourly between 8am and 3pm departing from Sorrento and between 9am and 4pm travelling to Sorrento.

On Saturdays it will run every two hours between 10.45am and 5pm departing from Sorrento and between 9.45am and 4pm travelling to Sorrento.

Route 787 – Sorrento – Rosebud departs from Sorrento Ferry Terminal and travels via Esplanade, Pt Nepean Rd, Ocean Beach Rd, Kerferd Av, Coppin Rd, Melbourne Rd, St Johns Wood Rd, Pt Nepean Rd, Adelaide St, William Rd, Kennedy Rd, Grant St, Canterbury Jetty Rd, Melbourne Rd, Dundas St and Nelson St to Rye Shopping Centre; then  via Nelson St, Lyons St, Sullivan St, Bimble St, Hygeia St, Point Nepean Rd, Ozone St, Bimble St, Government Rd, Field St, Alma St, Morris St, Yolland St, Darvall St, Barry St, Truemans Rd, Broadway, Eliza St, Swans Way, Elizabeth Av, Hakea Av, Cootamundra Av, Village Glen Dr, Chapel Dr, Village Glen Dr, Eastbourne Rd, Mirriam Av, Johnson St, Brendel St, Braidwood Av, Boneo Rd, The Drive, Warranilla Av, Windella Av, Thomas St and McCombe St to Rosebud Plaza.

To plan your journey for Route 787, visit Journey planner or call 1800 800 007 to order a printed copy of your timetable.
