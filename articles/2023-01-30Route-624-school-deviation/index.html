Route 624 school deviation

From Monday 30 January 2023, Route 624 will make one AM and one PM route deviation to drop off and pick up students attending Ashwood High School.

The deviation will operate on school days only.

An existing Route 624 deviation operates to Ashwood High School and will remain in place.

AM deviation

  • In the morning, the 8:14am Route 624 service from Oakleigh Station to East Kew Terminus will deviate to Ashwood High School.
  • As a result of the deviation, the morning timetable for the 8:14am Route 624 service will change.

PM deviation

  • In the afternoon, the 3:20pm Route 624 service from Chadstone Shopping Centre to Oakleigh Station will depart earlier to allow for the deviation to Ashwood High School.
  • The service will now depart from Chadstone Shopping Centre at 3:15pm.
  • As a result of the deviation, the afternoon timetable for the 3:15pm Route 624 service will change.

Passengers should check their timetable before they travel.

To plan your journey, view the new timetable for Route 624, visit Journey planner or call 1800 800 007 to order a printed copy of your timetable.
