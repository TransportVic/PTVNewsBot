National 4x4 Outdoors Show

The National 4x4 Outdoors Show is back! Three massive days to explore thousands of products from the industry's leading outdoor adventure and lifestyle brands. An opportunity to explore off-road camper trailers, caravans and tents, new model 4WDs, camping gear and much more.

The event will run: 

  • Friday 22 August: 9am to 5pm
  • Saturday 23 August: 9am to 5pm
  • Sunday 24 August: 9am to 4pm

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Please visit the event website for further event Information here.

Friday 22 August 2025

Metropolitan train

Getting to and from Melbourne Showgrounds

Trains will operate from Flinders Street (Platform 8/9), Southern Cross (Platform 13/14), stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 5/6), then express to Melbourne Showgrounds (Platform 1) in both directions. 

  • From 8:50am, services will operate every 40 minutes.

The last train to depart from Melbourne Showgrounds will depart at 3:49pm.

Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from Melbourne Showgrounds via Route 57

  • Trams will operate via Route 57 between Stop 01 Flinders Street / Elizabeth Street, stopping via Stop 32 Sandown Road/ Epsom Road via Flemington Road for passengers wishing to alight for the show.

Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Melbourne Showgrounds. 
The following bus route travels close to Melbourne Showgrounds: 

  • Route 472 (Williamstown to Moonee Ponds via Footscray)

Passengers can catch buses from Footscray Station and alight at Langs Road/Showgrounds. 

Plan your journey using Journey Planner. 

Regional train

Getting to Melbourne Showgrounds

Passengers arriving at North Melbourne and Southern Cross stations are advised to change trains and catch a Melbourne Showgrounds service. 

Refer to the Metropolitan trains section above for service times and platform information. 

Plan your journey using Journey planner. 

Leaving the Melbourne Showgrounds

Passengers are advised to catch any train from Melbourne Showgrounds and alight at Southern Cross or North Melbourne stations and change to a regional train.

Refer to the Metropolitan train section above for service times.
