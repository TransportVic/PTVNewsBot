Timetable improvements for Route 606

From Sunday 19 May 2024, Route 606 Elsternwick Station – Fishermans Bend will have an improved timetable with more services, longer operating hours and better connections with trains.

The new timetable will provide an additional 111 services each week on top of the 1380 services added to Fishermans Bend since 2022.

Weekday frequencies will increase, with most services operating every 30 minutes. On weekends, services will continue to operate every 60 minutes.

Operating hours will be extended every day of the week. Weekday services will operate between 6am and 9pm, Saturday services will operate between 7am and 9pm and Sunday services will operate between 8:30am and 9pm.

All weekend trips will be extended to Fishermans Bend, providing better connections to residential areas beyond Port Melbourne.

The once-daily deviation down Williamstown Road and Todd Road will be removed and services will be realigned via Salmon Street.

There will be better coordination with trains on weekdays at Elsternwick Station, make transfers easier and more convenient for passengers.
