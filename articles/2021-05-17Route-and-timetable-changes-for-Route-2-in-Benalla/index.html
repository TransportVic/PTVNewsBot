Route and timetable changes for Route 2 in Benalla

From Monday 17 May, bus Route 2 Benalla - Benalla East will be extended, providing transport connections for passengers travelling to Yooralla Business Enterprise and other businesses in the industrial estate east of Benalla’s town centre.                                                                                                                         

The route will be extended from Witt Street with Route 2 services running along Sydney Road and Enterprise Drive before turning around at Irwin Road, returning to Witt Street, then completing the normal route.

Four new accessible bus stops have been constructed along the extended route, including one in front of Benalla Tourist Park to provide seamless transport to Benalla town centre.

A new timetable will be in place to allow for the route deviation along Sydney Road.
