Route 760 Seaford Station to Cranbourne Station

A new route between Seaford to Cranbourne makes it easier for residents to travel to neighbouring suburbs and access shopping and leisure activities.

Route 760 will connect with trains at Seaford and Cranbourne stations giving onward public transport options on the metropolitan and regional services.

The service will stop at Flinders Christian College in Skye and St Peters Catholic Co-Ed College in Cranbourne West. Students living in Cranbourne and going to school at Carrum Downs Secondary College will also be able to connect with local buses servicing the school. 

Route 760 will start from Sunday 16 June and run seven days a week from 6am to 9.30pm Monday to Friday, 7am to 10pm on Saturdays and 8am to 9pm on Sundays.

The new timetable is available online and in Journey Planner. To order a printed copy, call 1800 800 007.
