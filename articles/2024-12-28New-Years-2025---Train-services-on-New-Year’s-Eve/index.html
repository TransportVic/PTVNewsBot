New Years 2025 - Train services on New Year’s Eve

Services on New Year’s Eve will operate to a Tuesday timetable, until the first timetable services on Wednesday 1 January 2025.

From 8pm to 11.30pm on New Year’s Eve, all lines will operate at a 10-20-minute frequency.

From 12.15am to 2.30am on Wednesday 1 January 2025, the following frequencies will apply to the lines below:

  • Mernda every 6 minutes
  • Hurstbridge every 6 minutes
  • Lilydale every 6 minutes
  • Belgrave every 6 minutes
  • Alamein every 20 minutes,
  • Glen Waverley every 6 minutes
  • Pakenham every 4 – 5 minutes
  • Cranbourne every 20 minutes
  • Frankston every 7 – 8 minutes
  • Sandringham every 7 – 8 minutes
  • Williamstown every 20 minutes
  • Werribee every 7 – 8 minutes (via the Altona Loop)
  • Sunbury every 7 minutes
  • Craigieburn every 7 minutes
  • Upfield every 15 minutes

From 2.30am to 3.30am, all lines will operate to a 20-minute frequency, connecting with outer services to:

  • Alamein via Camberwell
  • Cranbourne via Dandenong
  • Williamstown via Newport

Please note, all services to Werribee will operate via the Altona Loop.

From 3.30am until the first service on Wednesday 1 January 2025, all lines will operate to a 30-minute frequency, connecting with outer services to:

  • Alamein via Camberwell
  • Cranbourne via Dandenong
  • Williamstown via Newport

Please note, all services to Werribee will operate via the Altona Loop.

From first service on Wednesday 1 January 2025, services will run to a Saturday timetable.

New Year’s Eve will be busy. Plan ahead using the PTV Journey planner. You can also download the free and fully accessible PTV mobile app for Android and iOS and access public transport information on the go.
