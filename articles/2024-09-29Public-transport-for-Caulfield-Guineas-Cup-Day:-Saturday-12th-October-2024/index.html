Public transport for Caulfield Guineas Cup Day: Saturday 12th October 2024

Events > Racing

Extra public transport services are running from Caulfield Racecourse for the Caulfield Guineas Cup Day on Saturday 12 October 2024. 

Gates open: 10:30am 

First race: 12:15pm 

Main Race: 4:30pm 

Last Race: 5:45pm

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Train services will operate from Caulfield (Platform 1) to Flinders Street Station (Platform 12) stopping all stations. 

Trains to depart from: 

  • 4:53pm until 6:53pm every 4 minutes, Caulfield to Flinders Street Station.
