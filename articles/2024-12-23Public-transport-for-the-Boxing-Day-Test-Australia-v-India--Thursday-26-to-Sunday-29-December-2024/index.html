Public transport for the Boxing Day Test Australia v India: Thursday 26 to Sunday 29 December 2024

The Boxing Day Test is one of the biggest days on Melbourne’s sporting calendar. This year’s series is between Australia and India, and the best way for you to enjoy this is to take public transport.  

Extra services will help you get to and from the MCG from Thursday 26 December to Sunday 29 December. 

The event begins at 10.30am  

  • Thursday 26 December
  • Friday 27, Saturday 28 and Sunday 29 December

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.
