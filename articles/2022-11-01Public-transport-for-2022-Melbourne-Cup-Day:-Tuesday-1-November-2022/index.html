Public transport for 2022 Melbourne Cup Day: Tuesday 1 November 2022

Get ready for the race that stops a nation at Flemington Racecourse!

With Melbourne Cup on Tuesday 1 November, the 2022 Melbourne Cup Carnival officially gets off and racing at Flemington Racecourse!

There will be additional services so you can get to and from the races easily.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Make your journey hassle-free and remember to top up your myki and have it ready to use before travelling.

Melbourne Cup - Tuesday 1 November 2022

All services will operate to a public holiday timetable, with additional services noted below.

  • Gates open: 8.30am
  • First Race: 10:45am
  • Feature Race: 3:00pm
  • Last Race: 5.15pm

 Metropolitan train

Train services on all lines will run to a Saturday timetable. There will be no Night Network services.

Getting to the Racecourse

Flinders Street Station services will depart from Platform 8/9 and run express to Flemington Racecourse:

  • From 8.15am to 9.29am, every 10 to 15 minutes
  • From 9.35am to 1.23pm, every 7 to 8 minutes
  • From 1.35pm to 5pm, every 10 minutes (services will also stop at Southern Cross - Platform 14 and North Melbourne - Platform 6)

Southern Cross Station services will depart from Platform 8 stopping at North Melbourne Station and then running express to Flemington Racecourse:

  • From 8.22am to 9.22am, every 10 to 15 minutes
  • From 9.30am to 1.33pm, every 8 to 9 minutes

Leaving the Racecourse

Services will depart Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1 and 2) and run express to North Melbourne (Platform 5), Southern Cross (Platform 13) and Flinders Street (Platform 8/9) stations:

  • From 1.36pm to 3.07pm, every 10 minutes
  • From 3.11pm to 6.59pm, every 4 to 5 minutes
  • From 7.09pm to 8.59pm, every 8 to 10 minutes

Please note: The last service to depart Flemington Racecourse will be at 8.59pm from Platform 1

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

Passengers for the Sunbury, Werribee and Williamstown lines can catch a one-way shuttle bus from Leonard Crescent (outside Flemington Racecourse Station) express to Footscray Station:

  • From 3pm to 8.30pm, every 5 minutes

 Metropolitan tram

Trams will operate to a Saturday frequency, with last tram approximately one hour earlier than usual, and there will be no Night Network services.

Getting to and from the Racecourse

Additional services will operate on Route 57a tram, from Elizabeth Street to Epsom Road (Melbourne Showgrounds):

These services will operator as a shuttle service and will stop all stops between Stop 1 Flinders St Station and Stop 19 Royal Children’s Hospital, then express to Stop 30 Flemington Racecourse

  • From 10am to 7.30pm
