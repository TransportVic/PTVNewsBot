Myki-angelo runner up tram stop art

Artwork by our young myki-angelo, Lenka, aged 11

The creativity and talent we saw were incredible, with more than 6,000 entries received.

After much deliberation, Charlie’s design was chosen as the winning artwork and will be featured on all Youth myki cards, but Lenka now has her artwork featured on a tram stop as one of our top-rated myki-angelos.

Lenka, aged 11, was inspired by “Train maps and how much I love travelling to the city” and developed her own unique take on a network map.

You can see Lenka’s artwork now at tram stop 10 outside Parliament Station on MacArthur Street.
