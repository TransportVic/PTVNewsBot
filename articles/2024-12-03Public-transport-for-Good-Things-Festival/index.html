Public transport for Good Things Festival

Good Things Festival returns to Melbourne at the Flemington Racecourse! Public transport is your best way to enjoy it and we're running extra services to help you get to and from Flemington Racecourse on Friday 6 December.

The event runs from 11:30am to 10pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Please visit the event website for further event information here.

 Metropolitan train

Getting to Flemington Racecourse

Trains run from Flinders Street Station (Platform 8/9), stopping at Southern Cross Station (Platforms 13/14) and North Melbourne Station (Platforms 5/6), then express to Flemington Racecourse.

  • From 10:40am, trains leave every 10 minutes.
  • From 12:50pm, trains leave every 20 minutes.

During these times, return trains run express to Flinders Street.

The final train from Flinders Street leaves at 3:30pm.

Leaving from Flemington Racecourse

Train run from Flemington Racecourse, stopping at North Melbourne Station (Platform 5) and Southern Cross Station (Platform 13), then Flinders Street Station (Platform 10).

  • From 8pm, trains leave Flemington Racecourse to Flinders Street every 15 minutes.
  • From 9pm, trains leave Flemington Racecourse to Flinders Street every 10 minutes.
  • From 10pm, trains leave Flemington Racecourse to Flinders Street every 5 to 6 minutes.

The last train from Flemington Racecourse leaves at 11:15pm.

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Route 57

  • From 11am to midnight, extra trams run via Route 57a from Stop 1 Flinders Street/Elizabeth Street and Stop 32 Sandown Road/Epsom Road via Flemington Road.

 Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

The following bus route travels close to Flemington Racecourse: 

  • Route 472 (Williamstown - Moonee Ponds via Footscray)

Catch a bus from Footscray Station to Langs Road/Showgrounds. 

The last bus may run before the end of the match.

 Regional train

Normal trains run to and from Southern Cross Station. Connect with metropolitan trains from North Melbourne and Southern Cross stations.
