AFL Grand Final Eve Public Holiday: Friday 27 September 2024

Events > Public holiday

There will be changes to public transport services on Friday 27 September during the AFL Grand Final long weekend. Plan ahead by using Journey planner or by downloading the free PTV app.

Ticketing

On public holidays, your daily myki fare is capped at $7.20 ($3.60 for concessions and seniors), allowing unlimited travel all day.

Note: While some concession holders enjoy free travel on weekends, this benefit does not apply to public holiday timetables. On public holidays, normal myki fare caps are in effect for all passengers, including seniors.

For more information on fares in metropolitan Melbourne and regional Victoria, visit our fares page.
