Public transport for Melbourne Cup: Tuesday 2 November 2021

Get ready for the race that stops a nation at Flemington Racecourse!

Additional services will help you get easily to and from the Lexus Melbourne Cup on Tuesday 2 November 2021.

Before you travel

Please check in every time you see QR codes at stations and stops and while you travel on trains, trams and buses (even if have already touched on with a registered myki).

We strongly encourage you to register your myki so that we can contact you if you have been exposed to coronavirus (COVID-19) on a public transport service. Register your myki now - it’s quick and easy.

When travelling on public transport you must wear a fitted face mask covering your nose and mouth, unless you have a lawful reason for not doing so, and ensure you wear it for your entire journey.

Please check the Disruptions page for notification of any service changes that may affect your journey to and from the event.

Lexus Melbourne Cup - Tuesday 2 November 2021

All services will operate to a public holiday timetable, with additional services noted below.

  • Gates open: 8.45am
  • First Race: 10:45am
  • Feature Race: 3:00pm
  • Last Race: 5.15pm

 Metropolitan train

Train services on all lines will run to a Saturday timetable. There will be no Night Network services.

Getting to the Racecourse

Trains will depart from Flinders Street Station (Platform 10) to Flemington Racecourse stopping at Southern Cross Station (Platform 14), North Melbourne (Platform 6) and express to Flemington Racecourse Station.

  • From 8.29am to 12.29pm, every 20 minutes

Leaving the Racecourse

Trains will depart from Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1) to Flinders Street Station (Platform 10), stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 5) and Southern Cross (Platform 13):

  • From 3.19pm to 7.59pm, every 20 minutes

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

The last train departs Flemington Racecourse Station at 7.59pm.

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from the Racecourse

Passengers can catch a Route 57 tram, alighting at Stop 29 Racecourse Road/Flemington Racecourse.

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

 Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from the Racecourse

The following bus route travels close to the Racecourse:

  • Route 404 (Footscray - Moonee Ponds via Newmarket)

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

 Regional train

Getting to the Racecourse

Passengers arriving at North Melbourne and Southern Cross stations are advised to change trains and catch a Racecourse service.

Refer to the Metropolitan trains section above for service times and platform information.

Leaving the Racecourse

Passengers are advised to catch any train from the Racecourse and alight at Southern Cross or North Melbourne stations and change to a regional train.
