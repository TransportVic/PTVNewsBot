Australia Socceroos v Curacao

The Socceroos return to AAMI Park this March to host the FIFA Series 2026.

The game will begin at 8:10pm

Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner within 14 days of this event, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Metropolitan train

Additional services for the following lines depart after the match:

  • Glen Waverley, Frankston, Sandringham, Newport, Craigieburn, Dandenong

City Circle buses will be in operation. 

Ringwood Group passengers are advised to alight on Glen Waverley services then transfer to their respective train connection from Burnley.

Werribee passengers are to alight on Newport services from Flinders Street (Platform 8), then transfer at Newport for a bus.

Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from AAMI Park

Route 70

  • Trams will operate from Flinders Street / Elizabeth Street (Stop 4) and Stop 7C MCG/John Cain Arena.

Metropolitan bus

Customers can catch the following buses close to the MCG:

  • Route 246

Please note, services may terminate prior to the end of the match.

Regional train

  • Normal services departing from Southern Cross Station.
