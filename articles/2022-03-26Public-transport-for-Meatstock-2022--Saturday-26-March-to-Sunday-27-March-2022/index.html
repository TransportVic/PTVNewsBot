Public transport for Meatstock 2022: Saturday 26 March to Sunday 27 March 2022

Events > Expos

Extra public transport services are running to and from Meatstock 2022 at Melbourne Showgrounds on Saturday 26 March & Sunday 27 March 2022.

Doors open: 11.00am (Saturday) / 10:00am (Sunday)

Before you travel

QR codes have been rolled out across the public transport network to help keep passengers safe. Please check-in every time you see a QR code and register your myki as well. The details you share are used to contact you when necessary, to prevent further spread of COVID-19 in the community.

When travelling on public transport you must wear a fitted face mask if you are eight years and over covering your nose and mouth, unless you have a lawful reason for not doing so, and ensure you wear it for your entire journey.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Saturday 26 March 2022

Metropolitan train

Getting to the Melbourne Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Southern Cross (Platform 8), to Melbourne Showgrounds stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 2).

The first train is to depart Southern Cross Station at

  • 10:45am until 4:45pm every 30 minutes.

Leaving the Melbourne Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Melbourne Showgrounds to Southern Cross, stopping at North Melbourne.

Trains to depart

  • 11:28am until 6:30pm every 30 minutes

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from the Melbourne Showgrounds

Tram services will operate along Route 57 from Elizabeth Street/Flinders Street (Stop 1) to Melbourne Showgrounds, from Stop 32 (Epsom Road/Sandown Road) walk approximately 200 meters to Showgrounds entrance.

Plan your journey using Journey planner.
