New Stawell town bus network

The Stawell bus network will be upgraded to give residents more services, six days a week. The new bus network will combine Stawell’s three bus routes into two higher frequency routes and introduce Saturday services.

The new timetable will have ten trips a day on each route, Monday to Friday, and five trips per route on Saturdays.

Expanded operating hours on both routes will give Stawell residents more choice of when to travel, including in the morning and afternoon peaks.

The new services will start from Monday 2 March 2020. To view your new timetables, follow the links below and enter the date you want to travel:

  • Route 1 Stawell to Stawell South
  • Route 2 Stawell to Stawell North
