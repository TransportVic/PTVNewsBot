Public transport for Crown Makybe Diva Stakes Race Day

Events > Racing

We're running extra services to and from Crown Makybe Diva Stakes Race day on Saturday 14 September.

Gates open at 11am.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

 Metropolitan train

To the Racecourse

Catch a train to Flemington Racecourse from Southern Cross Station (Platform 8) or North Melbourne Station (Platform 2).

Trains leave:

  • Every 20 minutes from 10:45am to 1:05pm.

From the Racecourse

Catch any train from Flemington Racecourse to Southern Cross or North Melbourne stations.

Trains run express to Southern Cross Station.

  • Every 35 minutes from 4:35pm to 5:45pm
  • Every 20 minutes from 5:45pm to 7:05pm

Last train leaves at 7:05pm.

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Catch a Route 57 tram to Stop 29 Racecourse Road/Flemington Racecourse.

 Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Catch a Route 404 bus to and from the Racecourse. The last service may run before the event ends.

 Regional train

Getting to Flemington Racecourse

Catch a train from North Melbourne or Southern Cross stations to Flemington Racecourse.

From Flemington Racecourse

Catch a train from Flemington Racecourse to North Melbourne or Southern Cross stations for regional trains.
