Bus changes are coming to Clyde and Clyde North

From Sunday 28 August, bus Routes 897 and 898 will be extended to Clyde North to provide better coverage and access to schools, shops and train stations.

Route 897 will be known as Clyde North to Lynbrook Station via Cranbourne Park Shopping Centre.
Route 897 will be extended another 2.2km along Pattersons Road with 11 new bus stops and terminate near Watercolour Boulevard, Clyde North. The service frequency and the operating hours will remain the same.

Route 898 will be known as Clyde North to Cranbourne Station via Cranbourne Park Shopping Centre. Route 898 will be extended a further 4.8km along Heather Grove with 21 new bus stops and terminate near Tuckers Road, Clyde North.
