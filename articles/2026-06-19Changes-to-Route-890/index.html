Changes to Route 890

On 12 July 2026, we’re changing Route 890 to travel to more parts of Dandenong South.

To plan your journey:

  • Download the PTV App
  • Use the journey planner
  • Check the Route 890 timetable.
  • or call 1800 800 007.

What’s changing?

  • Route 890 will travel along Kirkham Road, Morwell Avenue and Allan Street in Dandenong South.
  • Route 890 will no longer travel along part of Hammond Road and Cheltenham Road.
  • The route change will give residents living on Kirkham Road, Morwell Avenue and Allan Street a frequent bus service closer to home.
  • Weekend services on Route 890 will finish one hour earlier at 8 pm.
  • Route 890 will have a new timetable.

Stop changes 

  • Some stops on Hammond Road and Cheltenham Road will no longer be used by Route 890.
  • If you catch Route 890 from Hammond Road or Cheltenham Road, you can catch routes 857 or 901 instead.
  • Route 890 will use existing bus stops on Kirkham Road and Morwell Avenue and service two new stops on Allan Street.


You should check the new Route 890 map and timetable and plan ahead before travelling.
