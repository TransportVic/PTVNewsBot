Public transport for the Cricket Big Bash League Melbourne Stars vs Sydney Sixers: Thursday 9 January 2025

It's time for a game of BBL cricket! Public transport is your best way to enjoy this, as the Melbourne Stars face off against Sydney Sixers to see who is going to qualify for the final series. Extra services will help you get to and from the MCG on Thursday 9 January.

The event will begin at 7.15pm.
