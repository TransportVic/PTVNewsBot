Run for the Kids

Extra Trains will run to Southern Cross Station prior to the event. 

Please allow extra time travelling to and from Southern Cross. Passengers are advised to disembark at Southern Cross and make their way to Docklands on foot, towards the start line on Footscray Road. 

There will be disruptions across the public transport and road network. Check for public transport and road disruptions before you leave home.

Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Metropolitan train

Getting to Southern Cross

Extra Trains will run to Southern Cross prior to the event, please use Journey Planner to plan your journey. 

Sunbury line passengers are advised to change trains at Footscray, then board a Craigieburn or Upfield line train to Southern Cross.

Pakenham and Cranbourne line passengers can alight at Town Hall and transfer to Flinders Street to board trains towards Southern Cross.

Please note there will be bus replacements running between:  

  • Newport to Werribee, all day
  • Camberwell to Ringwood, all day

Werribee line passengers are advised to board bus replacements and then alight at Newport to board trains to Southern Cross.

Lilydale and Belgrave line passengers are advised to board bus replacement services at Ringwood, then alight at Camberwell for a Glen Waverly or Alamein line train to Southern Cross.

Shuttle Services
