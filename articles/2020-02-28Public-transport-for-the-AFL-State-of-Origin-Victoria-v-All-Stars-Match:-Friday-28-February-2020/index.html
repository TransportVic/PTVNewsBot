Public transport for the AFL State of Origin Victoria v All Stars Match: Friday 28 February 2020

Extra public transport is running for all AFL fans for the Bushfire Appeal AFL State of Origin game between Victoria and the All Stars!

It's anticipated 53,000 people will be attending this unique game, with the first bounce at 7.50pm.

Don't forget the night kicks off with the AFLW operner between Collingwood and Melbourne at 5:40pm.

Please be aware of the following disruptions that may affect your journey:

  • Cranbourne and Pakenham lines: Buses replace trains from 8.10pm Friday 28 February to last train Sunday 1 March

  • Frankston Line: Buses replace trains between Flinders Street, Parliament and Moorabbin from 8pm Friday 28 February to 9pm Saturday 29 February

  • Frankston Line: Buses replace trains Flinders Street, Parliament and Moordialloc from 9pm Saturday 29 February to last train Sunday 1 March

  • Werribee Line: Buses replace trains between Newport and Werribee from 8.20pm Friday 28 February to 6.30am Saturday 29 February

  • Bairnsdale and Traralgon lines: Coaches replace trains from Friday 28 February to Sunday 1 March 

Please also check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey before you travel.
