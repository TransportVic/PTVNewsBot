Public transport for the A-League Round 22 – Melbourne Victory v Central Coast Mariners: Saturday 8 March 2025

Events > Sport

A-League continues at AAMI Park as Melbourne Victory takes on Central Coast Mariners.

Public transport is your best way to enjoy the action and we're running extra services to help you get home from AAMI Park on Saturday 8 March.

Kick-off is at 7:35 pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Note: Buses replace trains on the Hurstbridge Line between Heidelberg and Eltham during this time.

Buses also replace trains on the Upfield Line between North Melbourne and Upfield during this time.
