Public transport for CommBank Matildas v Argentina: Friday 30 May 2025

Events > Sport

Australia’s beloved Matildas are set to light up the field once again this May, taking on Argentina in what promises to be an action-packed international friendly.

This is a rare chance for local fans to see some of the biggest names in women’s football live on home soil. Expect fierce competition, spectacular goals, and plenty of green and gold pride as the Matildas continue to inspire a new generation of football fans across the country.

Getting home from Southern Cross

Passengers are to be advised regarding tram track and overhead works along St Georges Road, impacting tram route 11 from 9pm Friday 30 May to last tram Sunday 1 June. Buses will replace Route 11 trams between Stop 12 St Vincent's Plaza/Victoria Parade and Stop 47 West Preston/Gilbert Road. Passengers can catch the Mernda line trains or route 86 trams as an alternative to get home from Southern Cross.

Customers can catch the tram routes 30a & 86a to get to Southern Cross and continue their journey by walking to Marvel Stadium.
