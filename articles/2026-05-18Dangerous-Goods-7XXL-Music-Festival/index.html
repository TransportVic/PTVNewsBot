Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner within 14 days of this event, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Getting to and from Melbourne Showgrounds

Metropolitan train

Extra trains will operate from Flinders Street, stopping at Southern Cross and North Melbourne in both directions, before running express to Showgrounds Station.

Metropolitan tram

Take regular Route 57 (West Maribyrnong – Flinders Street Station).

Extra Route 57A (Flinders Street/Elizabeth Street – Epsom Road/Maribyrnong Road) operates on between 11:30am and 11pm.

Alight at stop 32 Showgrounds Village Shopping Centre on Epsom Road.

Metropolitan bus

Take Route 472 (Williamstown to Moonee Ponds) and alight at RAS Showgrounds/Langs Road.

Take Route 404 (Footscray to Moonee Ponds) and alight at Epsom Road/Ascot Vale Road.

Regional train

V/Line passengers arriving at Southern Cross Station should change to a train going to Showgrounds station.
