Victorian Vehicle Registration Rebate

This will save the average driver up to $186 per vehicle (maximum two) on their 2025-26 car registrations, helping ease cost of living pressures.

You’ll be able to apply for the rebate via the Service Victoria App or Service Victoria website from 1 June to 31 July 2026.

The rebate applies to more than 4 million privately registered light vehicles including cars, motorcycles, utes, vans, light trucks, station wagons, and 4WD wagons.

The rebate will be limited to two vehicles per person and will calculate reduced amounts for concession card holders.

More information will be available soon on the Service Victoria website.
