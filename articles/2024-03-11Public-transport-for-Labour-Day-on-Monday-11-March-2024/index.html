Public transport for Labour Day on Monday 11 March 2024

Events > Public holiday

The Labour Day public holiday falls on Monday 11 March 2024. Public transport services will mostly run to a Saturday timetable, but some services may be altered or will not run.

During public holidays, your daily myki fare is capped at $7.20 ($3.60 for concessions and seniors). You can enjoy all-day unlimited travel.

While some concession holders may benefit from free travel on weekends, please note this entitlement does not extend to public holiday weekend timetables. During public holidays, normal myki fare caps apply to all passengers, including seniors.

For more information, visit our fares and concessions pages.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner or view timetables for your line or route before you travel. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

 Metropolitan train

Trains will run to a Saturday timetable, with no Night Network services.

There may be planned works that could affect your travel. Check Disruptions before you travel.

 Metropolitan tram

Trams will operate to a Saturday frequency with no Night Network services.

This means that if your tram normally runs every seven minutes, it will continue to do so. However, times may differ slightly, with the last tram approximately one hour earlier than the normal Saturday timetable.

Most Metropolitan buses will run to a Saturday timetable.
