BBL match Melbourne Stars v Hobart Hurricanes

The Big Bash League is back for another season – bringing all the colour and excitement to the ‘G. Extra services will help you get to and from the MCG on Thursday 18 December.

The event will begin at 7.15pm.

Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner within 14 days of this event, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Metro Trains Additional services

Additional services for the following lines depart after the game:

  • Mernda, Greensborough, Flinders Steet, Ringwood, Glen Waverley, Frankston

Pakenham / Cranbourne Passengers are to alight on any available service from Richmond (Platforms 3, 7 & 8) or Jolimont (Platform 1), then transfer at Flinders Street (Platforms 4 & 5) for a City Circle service to Parliament for a bus (TBC).

Sandringham Passengers are to alight on Frankston services from Richmond (Platform 4), then transfer at South Yarra for a bus.

Werribee / Williamstown / Sunbury Passengers are to alight on any available service from Richmond (Platforms 3, 7 & 8) or Jolimont (Platform 1), then transfer at Flinders Street for a Northern Group service to North Melbourne for a bus.
