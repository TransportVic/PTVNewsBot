Bus timetable changes for Moe and Newborough

From 23 June 2019, the hours of coverage will increase on routes 14 and 15 on weekdays and weekends. Services on Route 14 will start earlier and run later each weekday, and start around the same time but run later on weekends.

Route 14 - Moe to Newborough via Old Sale Road

Services will start two hours earlier at 6.10am (previously 8.10am) Monday to Friday, with last service an hour later at 7.10pm (Monday to Thursday). On Friday nights, services will be extended three hours (from 6.10pm) until the last service at 9.10pm.

On Saturday, services will begin at 7.10am with the last service departing one hour later than previously at 6.10pm.

On Sunday services will begin at 8.10am and will be extended by two hours with the last service departing at 6.10pm.

View the new Route 14 timetable.

Route 15 - Moe to Newborough via Dinwoodie Drive 

Services will start at 6.43am Monday to Friday and be extended one hour later Monday to Thursday, with the last service departing 6.43pm. On Friday nights, services will be extended one hour with the last service departing at 9.43pm (instead of 8.40pm).

On Saturday, services will begin around the same time at 7.43am (previously 7.40am) and be extended one hour later with the last service departing at 6.43pm.

On Sunday services will begin around the same time at 8.43am (previously 8.40am) with last service departing two hours later than previously at 6.43pm.

View the new Route 15 timetable.
