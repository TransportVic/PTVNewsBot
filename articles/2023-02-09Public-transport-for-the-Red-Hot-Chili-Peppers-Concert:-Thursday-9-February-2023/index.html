Public transport for the Red Hot Chili Peppers Concert: Thursday 9 February 2023

Legendary rock band Red Hot Chili Peppers is returning to Marvel Stadium. Public transport is your best way to enjoy it, to see the Red Hot Chili Peppers. Extra services will help you get home from Marvel Stadium on Tuesday 9 February.

The gates open at 5:00pm.
