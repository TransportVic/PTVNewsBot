Public transport for the BBL cricket match Melbourne Stars v Hobart Hurricanes: Sunday 19 January 2025

Events > Sport

It's time for a game of BBL cricket! Public transport is your best way to enjoy this, as the Melbourne Stars face off against Hobart Hurricanes to see who is going to qualify for the final series. Extra services will help you get to and from the MCG on Sunday 19 January.

The event will begin at 7.15pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

 Metropolitan train

Please note that there will be bus replacements running between:

    • Reservoir to Mernda, all day. Mernda passengers are advised to board Reservoir services from Jolimont (Platform 2), then transfer at Reservoir for a bus.
  •  
    • North Melbourne to Craigieburn, from 9pm. Craigieburn Passengers are to board any available service from Richmond (Platforms 1, 3, 5, 7 & 8), then change at Flinders Street for Northern Group services to North Melbourne.
