Public transport for "Symbiotic Transmission” Festival: Sunday 10 March 2024

Events > Music

Get ready to immerse yourself in the electrifying extravaganza of Transmission, an unparalleled electronic music event unlike anything you’ve ever experienced. Prepare to be dazzled by a spectacular show that combines cutting-edge technology, mind-blowing special effects, captivating live acts, and a meticulously designed scenography that will leave you breathless and craving for more

A brand new venue in the heart of Flemington that redefines event experiences at Flemington Racecourse and extra public transport services are running to and from Flemington Racecourse on Sunday 10 March 2024.

Gates open: 3pm

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Make your journey hassle-free and remember to top up your myki and have it ready to use before travelling.

 Metropolitan train

Getting to the Racecourse

Train services will operate from Flinders Street Station (Platform 10) to Flemington Racecourse stopping at Southern Cross Station (Platform 14), North Melbourne (Platform 6), and then to Flemington Racecourse Station (Platform 1).

The first train is to depart Southern Cross Station

  • 2.39pm until 5.54pm every 15 minutes

Leaving the Racecourse

Train services will operate from Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1) to Flinders Street Station, stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 5), Southern Cross Station (Platform 13) and then into Flinders Street.

Trains to depart from:

  • 9:00pm until 10:30pm every 20 minutes
  • 10:30pm until 11:48pm every 10 minutes (Platform 1 only)

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from the Racecourse

Passengers can catch a Route 57 tram, alighting at Stop 29 Racecourse Road/Flemington Racecourse.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.

 Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from the Racecourse

The following bus route travels close to Flemington Racecourse:

  • Route 404 (please note service may finish prior to the end of the raceday)

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.

 Regional train

Getting to the Racecourse

Passengers arriving at North Melbourne and Southern Cross stations are advised to change trains and catch a Racecourse service.

Refer to the Metropolitan train section above for service times and platform information.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.

Leaving the Racecourse

Passengers are advised to catch any train from the Racecourse and alight at Southern Cross or North Melbourne stations and change to a regional train.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.
