Planned updates to the PTV app

Travelling in Victoria > App

We're making minor software updates to the PTV app on Thursday 29 August. You can use the app as normal during this time.

We'll prompt you to update your app when we've completed the upgrades. Download the latest version of the PTV app in Google Play or the App Store to continue using the app.

Your mobile device needs iOS 13 or higher, or Android 8.0 Oreo or higher, to run the PTV app.
