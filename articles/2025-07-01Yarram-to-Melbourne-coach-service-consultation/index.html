Yarram to Melbourne coach service consultation

We heard from locals and visitors on how to increase services on the V/Line Coach services between Yarram, Leongatha, Koo Wee Rup and Melbourne.

Your feedback is being reviewed and will help us provide coach services that better meet the public transport needs of the area.

The consultation was held from May 27 until June 29, 2025.

The online consultation was held on Engage Victoria. We also held local pop-up sessions in Yarram on 12 June, and in Leongatha and Koo Wee Rup on 17 June.

Any future changes to the V/Line coach service are planned to be introduced in 2026.
