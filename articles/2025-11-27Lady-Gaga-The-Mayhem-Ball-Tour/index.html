Lady Gaga The Mayhem Ball Tour

Gates Open: 5:00pm 

Show commences: 7:45 pm 

Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Friday 5 December

Metropolitan train

Additional services for the following lines depart after the concert:

  • Mernda, Greensborough, Ringwood, Glen Waverley, Dandenong, Frankston, Sandringham, Werribee, Craigieburn and Sunbury passengers.

Werribee passengers are to alight on Newport services from Southern Cross (Platform 14), then transfer at Newport for a connecting bus.

Upfield passengers are to alight on any available Northern Group service from Southern Cross (Platforms 11 & 14), then change at North Melbourne for a bus.

Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from Marvel Stadium

Extra trams will operate as Route 30a via La Trobe Street, and as Route 86a via Bourke Street. 

Take advantage of the city’s Free Tram Zone which extends from Queen Victoria Market to Docklands, Spring Street, Flinders Street Station and Federation Square. 

Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Marvel Stadium

Customers can catch the following bus routes close to Marvel Stadium:

  • Route 232 (Altona North to City)
  • Route 235 (Fishermans Bend to City)
  • Route 302 (Box Hill to City)
  • Route 304 (Doncaster SC to City)
  • Route 905 (The Pines SC to City)
  • Route 906 (Warrandyte to City)
  • Route 907 (Mitcham to City)

Please note, services may terminate prior to the end of the show.

Regional train

Additional services for the following lines arrive before the concert:

  • Waurn Ponds, Melton line

Additional services for the following lines depart after the concert:

  • Waurn Ponds, Wendouree, Ballarat & Bendigo 

Return coaches for the following lines depart after the concert:

  • Seymour Line
