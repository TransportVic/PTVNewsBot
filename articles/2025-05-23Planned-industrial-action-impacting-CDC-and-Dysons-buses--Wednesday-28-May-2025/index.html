Planned industrial action impacting CDC and Dysons buses: Wednesday 28 May 2025

News > Disruption

We are aware of planned industrial action impacting bus services operated by Dysons and CDC on Wednesday 28 May potentially affecting a significant number of routes across Melbourne as well as bus services in Geelong, Mildura and Ballarat.

The Department of Transport and Planning is working with Dysons and CDC to minimise impacts to passengers as much as possible.

Detailed information on impacted services will be available early next week. Please continue to check ptv.vic.gov.au for regular updates before travelling.
