Changes to timetables for metropolitan and regional bus services

From Sunday 28 February 2021, changes will be made to the timetables of many bus routes in metropolitan Melbourne as well as in regional areas including Geelong, Ballarat, Bendigo and Traralgon. The changes complete the second stage of re-coordinating more than 360 bus and coach routes with Victoriaâ€™s new train timetables.

You can now access the new coach and bus timetables for the re-coordinated services on our Timetables page, order a printed copy of your timetable by calling 1800 800 007 or use the Journey planner to plan ahead.

More bus timetables in metropolitan Melbourne will change on 11 April to fully align all bus routes that coordinate with trains with the new rail timetable. These bus timetables will be available ahead of the change.

Changes to train timetables from Sunday 31 January could affect your connecting bus journey. Please plan ahead before you travel using our Journey planner.

You can learn more about the first stage of bus re-coordination at changes to V/Line and bus services.
