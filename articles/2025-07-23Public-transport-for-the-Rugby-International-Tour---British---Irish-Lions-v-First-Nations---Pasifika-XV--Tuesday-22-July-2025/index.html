Public transport for the Rugby International Tour – British & Irish Lions v First Nations & Pasifika XV: Tuesday 22 July 2025

Events > Sport

For the first time in 12 years, The British & Irish Lions Tour will return to Australia for a festival of Rugby. In a historic clash, The British & Irish Lions will play the First Nations & Pasifika XV at Marvel Stadium on Tuesday, 22 July 2025.

Kick-off is at 7:45 pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

 Metropolitan train

Please note buses will be replacing metro trains running between:  

  • Heidelberg and Hurstbridge
  • Heidelberg and Eltham
  • Newport and Werribee

Hurstbridge passengers are advised to board a Heidelberg train from Platform 9 at Southern Cross station, then change at Heidelberg for a bus to continue their journey.

Werribee passengers are advised to board any train to Newport from Platform 14 at Southern Cross station, then change at Newport for a bus to continue their journey.

For the latest travel information, please listen to station announcements and check display screens, especially when making your way home.
