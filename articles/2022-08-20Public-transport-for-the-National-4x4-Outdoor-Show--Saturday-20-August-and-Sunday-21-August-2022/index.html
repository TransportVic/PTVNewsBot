Public transport for the National 4x4 Outdoor Show: Saturday 20 August and Sunday 21 August 2022

Extra public transport services are running to and from the National 4x4 Outdoor Show at Melbourne Showgrounds on Saturday 20 August and Sunday 21 August 2022.

Doors open at 9am each day.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Before you travel

We strongly encourage you to register your myki. With a registered myki you can protect your myki balance if the card is lost or stolen, check your travel history and manage multiple myki cards in the one account.

When travelling on public transport you must wear a fitted face mask covering your nose and mouth unless you have a lawful reason for not doing so, and ensure you wear it for your entire journey. 

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Metropolitan train

Saturday 20 August 2022

Getting to the Melbourne Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Southern Cross (Platform 8), to Melbourne Showgrounds stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 2). 

Trains depart Southern Cross Station from:

  • 8:44am until 5:14pm every 30 minutes

Leaving the Melbourne Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Melbourne Showgrounds to Southern Cross, stopping at North Melbourne.

Trains to depart:

  • 8:59am until 6:16pm every 30 minutes

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

Sunday 21 August 2022

Getting to the Melbourne Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Southern Cross (Platforms 8) to Melbourne Showgrounds, stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 2).

Trains depart Southern Cross Station from:

  • 8:44am until 4:14pm every 30 minutes.

Leaving the Melbourne Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Melbourne Showgrounds to Southern Cross, stopping at North Melbourne.

Trains to depart

  • 8:59am until 5:15pm every 30 minutes

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from the Melbourne Showgrounds

Passengers can catch a Route 57 tram, alighting at Melbourne Showgrounds from Stop 32 (Epsom Road / Sandown Road) and walk approximately 200 meters to Showgrounds entrance.
