Public transport for AFL Preliminary Final Geelong v Brisbane: Saturday 21 September 2024

The AFL Grand Preliminary Final is on in Melbourne! Public transport is your best way to enjoy it, as Geelong take on Brisbane to qualify for Grand Final glory! Extra services will help you get to and from the MCG on Saturday 21 September. The event will start at 5.15pm. 

Plan your trip to the game using Journey planner.

Before you travel

We strongly encourage you to register your myki. With a registered myki you can protect your myki balance if the card is lost or stolen, check your travel history and manage multiple myki cards in the one account.

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.
