Public transport for Caulfield Cup Day: Saturday 19 October 2024

Extra public transport services are running from Caulfield Racecourse for the Caulfield Cup Day on Saturday 19 October 2024.

Gates open: 10.30am

First race: 12.15pm 

Main Race: 4.30pm 

Last Race: 5.45pm 

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

 Metropolitan train

Leaving the Racecourse

Train services will operate from Caulfield (Platform 1) to Flinders Street Station (Platform 10) stopping all stations. 

Trains to depart from:

  • 4:52pm until 6:51pm every 20 minutes, Caulfield to Flinders Street Station.

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from the Racecourse

Passengers can catch a Route 3/3a tram, alighting at Stop 56 Caulfield Racecourse/Normanby Road.
