Transport Victoria: a central place for transport information

We’ve made it easier for everyone to access transport information. The PTV website is now part of the Transport Victoria website, creating a single place for transport information, bringing together public transport, roads and active transport.  

You will now find real-time information, journey planning tools and myki services conveniently located on the Transport Victoria website.

The website will continue to evolve over time, with new features to enhance the transport experience.

What you need to know

Why we have made this change

We’ve listened to customer feedback asking for a simpler, more seamless experience with transport information. The Transport Victoria  website brings together information on public transport, roads, and active transport in one central place.  

In addition, the old PTV website was nearing the end of its life and would have required further reinvestment to maintain. By bringing public transport information under transport.vic.gov.au, we’re reducing duplication and making it easier for you to travel around our great State.

Visit VicRoads for registration and licensing services

Visit VicRoads to complete registration and licensing tasks such as:

  • renewing a driver’s licence
  • getting your probationary driver licence
  • managing vehicle registration
  • transferring, buying or selling a vehicle or vessel.
