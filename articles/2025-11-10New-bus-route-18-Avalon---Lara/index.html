New bus route 18 Avalon - Lara

In early 2026, we’re introducing a new bus service between Avalon Airport and Lara Station.  

The new Route 18 will be the first public bus service in Avalon and will run seven days a week.

Route 18 will make it easier for travellers and workers to get to and from the airport and the Greater Avalon Employment Precinct.

This new service will complement existing Skybus and MyBus services.

Timetable and more information will be available on this page in 2026.
