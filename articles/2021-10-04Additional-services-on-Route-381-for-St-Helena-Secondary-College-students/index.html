Additional services on Route 381 for St Helena Secondary College students

From Monday 4 October 2021, additional AM and PM services will be introduced on Route 381 Mernda Station to Diamond Creek Station, making it easier for St Helena Secondary College students to get to and from the school.

The new Route 381 services will provide an additional morning and afternoon trip to and from the school, operating Monday to Friday on school days only.

The additional morning service will arrive at the school at 8:15am, 15 minutes before the school bell. The additional afternoon service will depart the school at 3:06pm, 15 minutes after the school bell.
