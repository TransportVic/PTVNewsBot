Timetable adjustments for improved travel on the Cranbourne Line

  • Route 791: Cranbourne Station – Frankston Station
  • Route 792: Cranbourne Station – Pearcedale
  • Route 795: Cranbourne Station – Warneet
  • Route 796: Cranbourne Station – Clyde
  • Route 798: Cranbourne Park SC – Selandra Rise
  • Route 799: Merinda Park Station – The Avenue Village Shopping Centre
  • Route 881: Merinda Park Station – Clyde North
  • Route 890: Dandenong Station – Lynbrook Station
  • Route 891: Lynbrook Station – Fountain Gate Shopping Centre
  • Route 892: Dandenong Station – Casey Central Shopping Centre
  • Route 893: Dandenong Station – Cranbourne Park Shopping Centre
  • Route 897: Lynbrook Station – Clyde
  • Route 898: Cranbourne Station – Cranbourne East

Coach services that connect to trains at Dandenong will also be adjusted to give passengers a more reliable service:

  • Cowes or Inverloch - Melbourne via Koo Wee Rup & Dandenong
  • Yarram or Leongatha to Melbourne

To plan your journey, visit the Journey Planner or order a printed copy of a timetable by calling 1800 800 007.
