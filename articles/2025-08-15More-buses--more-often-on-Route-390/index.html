More buses, more often on Route 390

These improvements make it easier to get to work, go shopping or catch the train at Craigieburn and Mernda stations.

To plan your journey:

  • Download the PTV app
  • Use the journey planner
  • Check the new Route 390 timetable
  • or call 1800 800 007.

What’s changing for Route 390 Mernda Station – Craigieburn Station via Wollert?

  • 140 new weekly trips.
  • On weekdays, services will run every 20 minutes between 6 am and 7 pm.
  • Weekday services will run later until 12 am, instead of 10:30 pm.
  • Saturday services will run later until 12 am, instead of 10 pm.
