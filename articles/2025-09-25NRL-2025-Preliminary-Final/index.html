NRL 2025 Preliminary Final

Experience the thrill of the 2025 NRL Finals Series as Melbourne Storm takes on Cronulla-Sutherland Sharks in a home Preliminary Final at AAMI Park. Public transport is your best way to enjoy the action.

Extra services will help you get home from AAMI Park.

Kick-off is at 7:50 pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey. 

Metropolitan train

Additional services for the following lines depart from Richmond Station after the match:

  • Ringwood, Glen Waverley, Dandenong, Frankston, Sandringham, Newport, Craigieburn and Sunbury passengers.

Newport Craigieburn and Sunbury specials will depart 30 minutes after the game from Flinders Street. Passengers are advised to catch any train from Richmond or Jolimont to Flinders Street Station, then transfer.

Werribee passengers are to alight on Newport services from Richmond (Platform 3) or Flinders Street (Platform 9), then transfer at Newport for a bus.
