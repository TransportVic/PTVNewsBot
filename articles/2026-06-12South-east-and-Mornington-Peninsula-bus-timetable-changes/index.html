South-east and Mornington Peninsula bus timetable changes

We’re introducing new timetables for 34 bus services across the south-east and Mornington Peninsula from 5 July 2026. Changes include additional trips on Route 833 between Frankston and Carrum Stations, and Routes 781, 784 and 785 providing more reliable connections with train services. 

Key Changes

 

 

Route 833 Frankston Station to Carrum Station via Carrum Downs From 5 July 2026

We’re introducing a new timetable for Route 833, with new weekly Saturday services and better train connections.

Key Changes

  • 15 extra Saturday trips each week.  
  • Saturday services will run every 40 minutes.  
  • Better connection with trains at Frankston Station
