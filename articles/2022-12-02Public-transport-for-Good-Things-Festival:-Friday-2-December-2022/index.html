Public transport for Good Things Festival: Friday 2 December 2022

Music festivals are back at Flemington Racecourse and extra public transport services are running to and from Good Things Music Festival at Flemington Racecourse on Friday 2 December 2022

Gates open: 12pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Make your journey hassle-free and remember to top up your myki and have it ready to use before travelling.

 Metropolitan train

Getting to the Racecourse

Train services will operate from Flinders Street Station (Platform 8/9) to Flemington Racecourse stopping at Southern Cross Station (Platform 14), North Melbourne (Platform 6), and then to Flemington Racecourse Station (Platform 1).

Trains depart Flinders Street Station from:

  • 11.31am until 3.50pm every 15-20 minutes

Leaving the Racecourse

Train services will operate from Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1) to Flinders Street Station (Platform 10), stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 5), Southern Cross Station (Platform 13) then into Flinders Street.

Trains to depart from:

  • 8.02pm until 9:59pm every 20 minutes
  • 10:10pm until 11.20pm every 10 minutes (from Platform 1 and 2)

Please note: The last service to depart Flemington Racecourse will be at 11.20pm

Regional coach

Getting to the Racecourse

Passengers arriving at Southern Cross Station coach terminal are advised to walk to Platform 14 and catch a Flemington Racecourse service.

Refer to the Metropolitan train section above for service times and platform information.

Leaving the Racecourse

Customers can catch any service departing Flemington Racecourse, alight at Southern Cross Station and then walk to the coach terminal.
