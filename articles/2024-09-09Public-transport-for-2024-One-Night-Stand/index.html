Public transport for 2024 One Night Stand

Events > Music

Get ready for One Night Stand at Friendly Societies Park in Warrnambool! We're running extra services on Saturday and Sunday to get you to and from the event. 

Friendly Societies Park, Warrnambool 

Gates open 2:30pm to 10:30pm 

Before you travel

Bookings are essential for V/Line trains to Warrnambool this weekend. Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

You must reserve a seat in advance to travel on V/Line services to Warrnambool.

You can make a reservation:

  • Online on the V/Line website and mobile app
  • By calling 1800 800 077
  • In person at a staffed V/Line station, V/Line ticket agent or a Metro premium station.

You need a V/Line paper ticket to travel to and from Warrnambool. Find out more about regional fares.

Visit the V/Line website to reserve your seat.

There is no late-night train from Warrnambool to Melbourne. If you are planning to travel back to Melbourne after the event, you will need to make other arrangements or stay overnight and return on Sunday. 

Please refer to the ABC website for further One Night Stand information.
