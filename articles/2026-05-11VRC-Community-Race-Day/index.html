Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner within 14 days of this event, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Metropolitan train

Trains will operate from Flinders Street (Platforms 6) in both directions, stopping at Southern Cross (Platforms 11/12) and North Melbourne (Platforms 4/3), then express to Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1). 

Trains will commence from Flinders Street from 10:50am and run until 1:20pm. 

Please note there will be a break in service between 1:21pm and 3:33pm.

Trains will commence from Flemington Racecourse from 3:34pm and run until 6:14pm. 

Metropolitan tram

Take Route 57 trams (West Maribyrnong – Flinders Street Station) and alight at Stop 30 Flemington Drive/Epsom Road.

Metropolitan bus

Take Route 404 (Footscray to Moonee Ponds) and alight at Epsom Road/Ascot Vale Road. 

Take Route 472 (Williamstown to Moonee Ponds) and alight at Flemington Racecourse/87 Fisher Parade. 

Regional train

V/Line passengers arriving at Southern Cross Station should transfer to a Flemington Racecourse service. Return trains also stop at Southern Cross Station. Please refer to the Metropolitan Trains section above for platform information.
