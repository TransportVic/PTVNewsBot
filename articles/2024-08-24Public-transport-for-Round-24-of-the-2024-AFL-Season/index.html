Carlton v St Kilda, Marvel Stadium, Sunday 25 August

  • La Trobe Street closed in both directions between Queen and Victoria streets
  • No city circle trams run along La Trobe Street.

Getting to and from Mars Stadium, Ballarat

Regional bus

Ballarat shuttle bus

Shuttle bus services will run from Ballarat Station Bus Terminal to Mars Stadium from 10am for patrons.

From 2:30pm shuttle bus services will operate from Mars Stadium and drop passengers off in Lydiard St North outside the train station, a second drop off will be made in Sturt Street opposite the Ballarat Town Hall.

Park & Ride Shuttle

The Park & Ride Shuttle service that has previously operated on AFL matches to and from the location in Doveton Street North will not be in operation.

Regional train

Ballarat line

Before the game

An extra train service leaves Southern Cross Station at 9:44am. This train will stop at Footscray, Sunshine, Deer Park, Caroline Springs, Rockbank, Cobblebank, Melton, Bacchus Marsh, Ballan, arriving at Ballarat Station at 11:17am.

After the game

An extra train service leaves Ballarat Station at 4:00pm. This train will stop at Ballan, Bacchus Marsh, Melton, Cobblebank, Rockbank, Caroline Springs, Deer Park, Ardeer, Sunshine, Footscray, arriving at Southern Cross Station at 5:31pm.
