Design the New Youth myki Card! We’re running a design competition!

Fares and tickets > Mobile myki

Calling all young artists aged 5 to 17, this is your chance to have your artwork featured on Victoria’s new Youth myki card, plus win a $1,000 Visa gift card! 

From 1 January 2026, public transport will be free for all Victorians 17 and under. That means kids and teens can hop on a train, tram or bus anywhere across Victoria at no cost.    

Got a great idea? Sketch it, paint it, or design it digitally, then enter for your chance to win.   

Entries open 6 June and close 15 June 2025.  

Submit your entry at engage.vic.gov.au/youthmyki
