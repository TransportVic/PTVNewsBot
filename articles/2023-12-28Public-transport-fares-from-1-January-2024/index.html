Public transport fares from 1 January 2024

News > Fares/Tickets

As of 1 January 2024, public transport fares in Victoria will increase by a weighted average of 5 per cent, in line with the annual Consumer Price Index (CPI) rise.

The daily full fare cap will increase to $10.60 or $5.30 for concessions. On a weekend or public holiday, daily fares will remain capped at $7.20 or $3.60 for concessions.

The increase will add 60 cents to the full fare daily Zone 1, Zone 1+2 metropolitan fares, and the regional fare cap. The regional fare cap ensures that fares across regional Victoria continue to be capped at the same amount as metropolitan daily fares.

For regional Victorians, the cost of getting around town will increase. The full fare regional town bus two-hour fare will increase by 20 cents to $2.80 and daily fare will increase by 40 cents to $5.60.

Public transport fares contribute to the ongoing operation of the trains, trams and buses on our network and ensure we network can continue to deliver a fair and sustainable service for all Victorians.
