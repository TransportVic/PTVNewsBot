Public transport works this spring

As part of Victoria’s Big Build, major construction has finished on three of the five new Metro Tunnel underground stations and trial operations will continue ahead of opening. Works continue to remove dangerous and congested level crossings. 

As the West Gate Tunnel nears completion, works continues to widen the West Gate Freeway from 8 to 12 lanes, while road upgrades are in progress across the north and south east as part of Big Build Roads. Tunnelling continues on the North East Link and upgrades are underway on the Eastern Freeway and M80 Ring Road.  
Early works will continue to prepare for tunnelling on Suburban Rail Loop East, while Yarra Trams track renewal will take place on various routes across the tram network. 

For more information about planned disruptions, visit Victoria’s Big Build. 

Major disruptions

Be sure to plan ahead and allow more time for travel during major works this autumn, especially if you’re travelling over the school holiday period.

To avoid delays:

  • plan your journey before you travel using our journey planner
  • allow extra travel time
  • consider alternatives if you can and travel outside of peak times where possible.

There are also smaller disruptions for essential works happening across the network.
