New buses for Eynesbury and Weir Views

From Sunday 8 December, we’re introducing a new bus service between Eynesbury and Melton Station via Weir Views. The new Route 452 will run seven days a week with weekday services every 15 – 30 minutes during busy periods and up to 40 minutes at other times. On weekends, Route 452 will run every 60 minutes.  

Route 452 will connect Eynesbury residents to public transport for the first time, with a direct service to Melton Station. This will make it easier to access trains, other buses and key destinations like Opalia Plaza, Woodgrove Shopping Centre and Melton Town Centre.  

The route map and timetable will be available from Friday 22 November.
