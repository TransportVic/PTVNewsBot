Cadel Evans Great Ocean Road Race

Cycling Events during the Cadel Evans Great Ocean Road Race:

  • Wednesday 28 January: Surf Coast Classic - Women, Lorne to Torquay
  • Thursday 29 January: Surf Coast Classic - Men, Lorne to Torquay
  • Friday 30 January: Family Ride, Geelong Waterfront
  • Saturday 31 January February: People’s Ride and Cadel Evans Great Ocean Road Ride – Women, Geelong-Bellarine-Surf Coast
  • Sunday 1 February: Cadel Evans Great Ocean Road Ride – Men, Geelong-Bellarine-Surf Coast

Please refer to Cadel Evans Great Ocean Roadrace website for further event information, road closures and accessibility information.

Planned Closures

Due to the Cadel Evans Great Ocean Road Race in Geelong, the Bellarine and Surf Coast, bus Routes 1, 20, 30, 31, 24, 41, 42, 43, 50, 52, 53, 54, 51, 55, 56, 60, 61 and 101 (Apollo Bay - Geelong) will experience delays, be diverted in both directions and selected bus stops will not be serviced on Wednesday 28 January, Thursday 29 January, Saturday 31 January and Sunday 1 February.

Additionally, on Saturday 31 January, the Warrnambool Line will be suspended between Waurn Ponds and Warrnambool, affecting the 1:10pm Southern Cross to Warrnambool and 5:36pm Warrnambool to Melbourne services. Replacement Coaches will be available from Geelong Station.
