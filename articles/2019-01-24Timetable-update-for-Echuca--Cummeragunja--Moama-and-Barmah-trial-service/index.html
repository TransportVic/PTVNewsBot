Timetable update for Echuca, Cummeragunja, Moama and Barmah trial service

A trial service between Echuca and Barmah, servicing the communities of Cummeragunja and Moama, was introduced on 24 January 2019.

From Thursday 31 October 2019, the service will have a new timetable to better meet community needs.

Services will depart Barmah at 10.35am and 3.15pm (instead of 9.35am and 4.15pm).

Services from Echuca will depart at 9.40am and 2.15pm (instead of 8.40am and 3.15pm).

The trial service operates every second Thursday. View a route map and updated timetable or plan your journey.

For further information or to request a printed timetable, call 1800 800 007.
