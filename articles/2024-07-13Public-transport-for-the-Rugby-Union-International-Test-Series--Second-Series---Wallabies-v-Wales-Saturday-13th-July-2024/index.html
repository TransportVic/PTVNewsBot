Public transport for the Rugby Union International Test Series: Second Series – Wallabies v Wales Saturday 13th July 2024

Events > Sport

Public transport is your best way to enjoy the Wallabies taking on the Wales for the International Test Series. Extra services will help you get to and from the AAMI Park on Saturday 13 July.

The event will begin at 7:45pm.
