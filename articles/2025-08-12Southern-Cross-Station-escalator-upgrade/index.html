Southern Cross Station escalator upgrade

The upgrade will take place in two stages, starting in late July 2025.

Early works (late July 2025 – late September 2025)

We’re doing maintenance works at both ends of platforms 9-14 to get ready for the major upgrade.  Works run from 8.30pm to 4.30am. During the works one escalator will go up and one escalator will go down.

Main works (8.30pm Monday 29 September 2025 – August 2026)

We’re closing one of the three escalators on platforms 9-14 during the main works.

  • Two escalators at the Collins Street end of each platform will operate at any one time.
  • Two escalators at the Bourke Street end of each platform will operate at any one time.
  • All platforms will remain open.

Plan ahead and allow extra time

Platforms will be busier than usual during weekday peaks (7am to 10am and 3pm to 7pm). 

Escalator operations will change during peak times. So will how you enter and exit the station. 

Follow the signage and directions from staff to help everyone safely enter and exit the platforms.

You could also consider travelling outside of the morning peak, travelling to another CBD station or catching a tram, if possible.

Collins Street escalator operations during weekday morning and afternoon peaks

Weekday morning peak (7am – 10am): No escalators will be going down at the Collins Street end of the platforms. Please use the Bourke Street entrance, a four-minute walk along Spencer Street from the Collins Street entrance.

Weekday afternoon peak (3pm -7pm): No escalators will be going up at the Collins Street end of the platforms. Use the Bourke Street end escalators to exit the platform.

At other times of the day (10am – 3pm and 7pm – 7am): One escalator will be running up and one will be running down.

Transiting passengers – use Bourke Street escalators

To interchange to a train on platforms 9 - 14, V/Line and metropolitan passengers should use the Bourke Street escalators to exit the platform.

Accessibility

Need help at Southern Cross Station? Travellers Aid can assist. Call Travellers Aid on (03) 9670 2072, book online here or email scs@travellersaid.org.au.

Lifts at both ends of the platform will be available throughout the works. Please give priority to the lifts to people with accessibility needs.

Affected train lines

  • Platforms 9/10: Mernda and Hurstbridge, Belgrave, Lilydale, Alamein and Glen Waverley lines.
  • Platforms 11/12: Craigieburn, Upfield and Sunbury, Cranbourne and Pakenham lines.
  • Platforms 13/14: Werribee and Williamstown, and Craigieburn lines.

About the escalator upgrade

New, stronger escalators will be installed to ensure the growing number of passengers who use Southern Cross Station can get where they need to go, quickly and safely.

The upgraded escalators will move faster, carry more people and be more reliable.

Thank you for your patience during the works.
