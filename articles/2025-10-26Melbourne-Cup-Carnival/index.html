Melbourne Cup Carnival

We're running extra services to get you to the 2025 Melbourne Cup Carnival, including: 

  • Howden Victoria Derby Day
  • Lexus Melbourne Cup Day
  • Crown Oaks Day
  • Stakes Day.

Train

Trains will run every 10 minutes in and out of Flemington Racecourse Station during the carnival. Use Journey Planner for your trip. 

Catch the train to Flemington Racecourse Station from:

  • Flinders Street Station
  • Southern Cross Station
  • North Melbourne Station.

Note there will be lots of travellers using Flemington Racecourse Station.
