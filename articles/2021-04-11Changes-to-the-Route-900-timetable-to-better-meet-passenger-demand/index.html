Changes to the Route 900 timetable to better meet passenger demand

From Sunday 11 April 2021 a new timetable for Route 900 - Stud Park Shopping Centre to Caulfield via Monash University and Chadstone - will better reflect passenger demand and will allow for higher frequency services when they are needed most. Buses will travel every 20 minutes on Saturday afternoons for services travelling from:

  • Stud Park Shopping Centre to Caulfield Station between 1.50pm and 4.50pm
  • Caulfield Station to Stud Park Shopping Centre between 2.50pm and 5.50pm

The change adds six extra services between Stud Park Shopping Centre and Caulfield Station during the busy Saturday afternoon period.

The frequency of weeknight services from Stud Park Shopping Centre to Caulfield Station will also change, reflecting lower passenger demand at this time. The service will change to a 15-minute frequency at 6.10pm instead of 7.10pm.

The timetable will coordinate with the new train timetable at Caulfield Station.
