Minor timetable changes for bus services in the Cranbourne area

From 8 December, passengers in the Cranbourne area will enjoy new and accessible stops along Narre Warren-Cranbourne Road.

To access the new stops, passengers on routes 795, 796, 798, 799, 841, 881, 893, 897, 898 & 981 will have minor timetable changes in some sections of their trips.

To plan your journey:

  • Download the PTV app
  • Use the journey planner at ptv.vic.gov.au
