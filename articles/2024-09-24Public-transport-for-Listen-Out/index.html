Public transport for Listen Out

Events > Music

Listen Out is on at Caribbean Gardens! Public transport is your best way to enjoy it. We're running extra services to help you get to and from the event on Friday 27th September.

The event starts at 12pm and concludes at 10pm.

Public transport runs to a Saturday timetable for the Grand Final Public Holiday on Friday 27 September.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

 Metropolitan train

To the event

Regular trains run to Ringwood Station.

From the event

Extra trains run from Ringwood and Glen Waverley Stations.

Extra return trains from Glen Waverley Station departing at:
  • 10:38pm, arriving Flinders Street Station at 11:12pm
  • 10:48pm, arriving Flinders Street Station at 11:22pm
Extra return trains from Ringwood Station Platform 2 departing at:
  • 10:29pm, arriving Flinders Street Station at 11:10pm
  • 10:49pm, arriving Flinders Street Station at 11:30pm

Shuttle bus

To the event

Catch a train to Ringwood Station and board a free event shuttle bus to Listen Out from 11am. Shuttles run one way only.

From the event

Free shuttle buses run from Listen Out to Ringwood and Glen Waverley Stations for trains to the City. Extra trains run from 10:29pm to the City to help you get home.

After the event, buses go to Ringwood and Glen Waverley train stations to speed up departures. We encourage choosing the one with the shortest queue!

Taxi/Ride Share

The drop off zone for all vehicles is on Dalmore Drive, accessible via Caribbean Drive from Ferntree Gully Road. The main entry is a short walk from the drop off zone.

We do not recommend driving to the event site. Parking close to the event site is extremely limited.

Entrance to the event is located at the end of Caribbean Drive, access is from Ferntree Gully Road.

Caribbean Gardens business park has traffic changes from 7am to midnight to ensure the safe arrival and departure of patrons. Traffic controllers manage this to ensure with the safe delivery of the event.  During these times, there will be more pedestrian and vehicle traffic in the local area. Expect short delays.

For more information on transport, visit the Listen Out website.
