Timetable changes for Eltham and Diamond Creek buses

From Sunday 8 November 2020, bus services in Eltham and Diamond Creek will have adjusted timetables. Changes to timetables will give passengers a more reliable service and improve connections with trains and school bell times.

View the new Eltham and Diamond Creek bus timetables below:

  • Route 343 Hurstbridge – Greensborough
  • Route 578 Eltham Station – Warrandyte (via Research and Kangaroo Ground)
  • Route 579 Eltham Station – Warrandyte (via Research)
  • Route 580 Diamond Creek – Eltham Station
  • Route 582 Eltham Town Service

We’re also adding extra services to Route 582 on weekdays to reduce wait times in the evening.  

School buses 

Current timetabled school deviations on routes 578, 579 and 582 have been revised and some school deviations will become school specials. Please contact your school directly for more details.
