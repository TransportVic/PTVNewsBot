Oaks Day -Thursday 9 November 2023

All services will operate to a normal Thursday timetable, with additional services as noted below.

  • Gates open: 10:30am
  • First Race: 12:40pm
  • Feature Race: 5:10pm
  • Last Race: 5:50pm

 Metropolitan train

Getting to the Racecourse

Flinders Street Station services will depart from Platform 8/9 stopping at Southern Cross Station (Platform 14), North Melbourne Station (Platform 6) and then running express to Flemington Racecourse:

  • From 10:14am to 10:43am, every 15 minutes
  • From 10:43am to 2:28pm, every 4 to 6 minutes
  • From 2:28pm to 3:33pm, every 10 minutes
  • From 3:33pm to 5:10pm, every 20 minutes

Leaving the Racecourse

Services will depart Flemington Racecourse (Platforms 1 and 2) and run express to North Melbourne (Platform 5), Southern Cross (Platform 13) and Flinders Street (Platform 8/9) stations only:

  • From 2:23pm to 3:21pm, every 10 minutes
  • From 3:38pm to 5.28pm, every 20 minutes
  • From 5:28pm to 7:52pm, every 5 minutes
  • From 7:59pm to 8:59pm, every 10 minutes

Please note: The last service to depart Flemington Racecourse will be at 8:59pm from Platform 1

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.

Footscray Station bus shuttle

Passengers for the Sunbury, Werribee and Williamstown lines can catch a one-way shuttle bus from Leonard Crescent (outside Flemington Racecourse Station) express to Footscray Station:

  • From 3:00pm to 8:00pm, every 5 minutes

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from the Racecourse

Additional services will operate on Route 57a from Elizabeth Street to Epsom Road (Melbourne Showgrounds):

These services will operator as a shuttle service and will stop all stops between Stop 1 Flinders St Station and Stop 19 Royal Children’s Hospital, then express to Stop 30 Flemington Racecourse

  • From 10:30am to 1:30pm and 4pm to 8pm
