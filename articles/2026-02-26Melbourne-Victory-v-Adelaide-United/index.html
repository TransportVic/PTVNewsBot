Melbourne Victory v Adelaide United

Experience the thrill of the A-League Men 2025-26 season as Melbourne Victory take on Adelaide United at AAMI Park. 

The game will begin at 7:35pm

Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner within 14 days of this event, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Metropolitan train

Additional services for the following lines depart after the match:

  • Ringwood, Glen Waverley, Frankston, Cheltenham, Sandringham

Craigieburn passengers are advised to alight on any available service from Richmond Platforms 1, 3, 7 and 8 to Flinders Street, then transfer to any available Northern Group service from either platforms 5 or 8 to then connect to their respective bus service from North Melbourne. 

Werribee passengers are advised to alight on any available service from Richmond Platforms 1, 3, 7 and 8 to Flinders Street and then transfer to a Newport service from platform 8 to transfer to their connecting bus service from Newport. 

Passengers wanting to alight on services departing Flinders Street are advised to alight on trains departing from Richmond or alternatively board Route 70 Trams. 

Metropolitan tram
