Kendrick Lamar - Grand National Tour

Kendrick Lamar will bring the Grand National Tour to Melbourne this summer at AAMI Park. 

Gates open at 5:30pm
Event begins at 7:00pm

Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Metropolitan train

Wednesday 3 December

Additional services for the following lines depart after the concert:

  • Ringwood, Glen Waverley, Dandenong, Caulfield, Sandringham, Newport(Werribee ), Craigieburn and Sunbury passengers.

Frankston line passengers:

  • Board train service to Caulfield at Richmond (Platforms 4 & 6)
  • Change at Caulfield to a connecting bus to Frankston.

Werribee line passengers:

  • Board train service to Newport at Richmond (Platform 3) or Flinders Street (Platform 9)
  • Change at Newport to a connecting bus to Werribee.

Thursday 4 December

Additional services for the following lines depart after the concert:

  • Ringwood, Glen Waverley, Dandenong, Caulfield, Sandringham, Newport, Craigieburn and Sunbury passengers.

Frankston line passengers:

  • Board train service to Caulfield at Richmond (Platforms 4 & 6)
  • Change at Caulfield to a connecting bus to Frankston.

Werribee line passengers:

  • Board train service to Newport at Richmond (Platform 3) or Flinders Street (Platform 9)
  • Change at Newport to a connecting bus to Werribee.
