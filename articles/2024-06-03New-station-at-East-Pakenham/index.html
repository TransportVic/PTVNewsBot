New station at East Pakenham

From Monday 3 June 2024, all services on the Pakenham Line will be extended to East Pakenham Station.

The first Saturday and Sunday service towards the city will depart two minutes later at all stations through to Flinders Street Station. These services will depart East Pakenham Station at 3:02 am, arriving at Flinders Street Station at 4:15 am.

The rest of the timetable between the city and Pakenham Station won’t change.

To plan your journey, view the Pakenham Line timetable, visit Journey planner, or call 1800 800 007 to order a printed copy of your timetable.

For more information, visit Victoria’s Big Build.
