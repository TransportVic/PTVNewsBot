Public transport for the A-League Men Grand Final – Melbourne City FC v Melbourne Victory: Saturday 31 May 2025

Events > Sport

Experience the excitement of the 2024-25 A-League Men Grand Final at AAMI Park, as Melbourne City FC takes on Melbourne Victory at AAMI Park. Public transport is your best way to enjoy the action.

Extra services will help you get home from AAMI Park on Saturday 31 May 2025.

Kick-off is at 7:40 pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Passengers on the Hurstbridge line, please note, buses replace trains between Clifton Hill and Eltham stations.
