Industrial action to disrupt bus services on Tuesday 1 July 2025

News > Disruption

We are aware of planned industrial action impacting bus services operated by CDC on Tuesday 1 July potentially affecting a significant number of routes across Melbourne as well as bus services in Geelong, Mildura and Ballarat.

The Department of Transport and Planning is working with CDC to minimise impacts to passengers as much as possible.

Detailed information on impacted services will be available later this week. Please continue to check ptv.vic.gov.au for regular updates before travelling.
