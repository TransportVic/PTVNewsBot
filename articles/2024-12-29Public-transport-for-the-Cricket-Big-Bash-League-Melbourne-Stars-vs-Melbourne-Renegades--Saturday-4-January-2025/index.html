Public transport for the Cricket Big Bash League Melbourne Stars vs Melbourne Renegades: Saturday 4 January 2025

Events > Sport

The Big Bash League is back for another season bringing all the colour and excitement to the MCG. There are some amazing fixtures – perfect for a New Year Party Bash in style!
