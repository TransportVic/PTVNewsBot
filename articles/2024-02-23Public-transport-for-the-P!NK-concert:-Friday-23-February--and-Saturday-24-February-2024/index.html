Public transport for the P!NK concert: Friday 23 February and Saturday 24 February 2024

Events > Music

Three-time Grammy Award-winning singer, performer, and international pop icon P!NK is coming to Melbourne with her highly anticipated return to Australia on her ’Summer Carnival’ stadium tour at Marvel Stadium on Friday 23 & Saturday 24 February 2024. Extra services will help you get home from Marvel Stadium on both days.

Gates open at 5pm.

  • Jump to Saturday 24 February 2024

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

The Marvel Stadium car park will remain available via Flinders Street while Wurundjeri Way and Dudley Street intersection is closed. Please note that car park allocation has now reached capacity for this event. Patrons that have pre-booked parking can access Car Park A and B via Bourke Street and Car Park C via the Flinders Street end of Wurundjeri Way.

Please allow extra time if travelling by car as significant delays are expected. Alternatively, extra public transport services are running to get you to and from the event.
