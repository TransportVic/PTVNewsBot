Croydon station bus interchange

Changes to buses at Croydon Station

From 10 November 2024, the new bus interchange at Croydon Station will open, making it easier and safer to connect to trains. 12 bus routes will use the new interchange. Routes 380 and 694 will also have changes.

Built as part of the Croydon Transport Hub and Coolstore Road level crossing removal project, the 14-bay interchange will become one of Melbourne’s largest facilities, with nearly 3000 weekly trips.

Timetables for these routes will change, but operating hours and service frequency will stay the same.

Route 380 changes

Route 380 Ringwood – Croydon Loop is being replaced by two new routes: 668 and 669.

Route 668 will run between Ringwood and Croydon via Burnt Bridge Shopping Centre. Route 669 will run between Ringwood and Croydon via East Ringwood Station. Together, these routes will form a loop, covering the same areas as Route 380.

When a Route 668 bus arrives at Croydon Station, its number will change to Route 669 and continue along the loop. When a Route 669 bus arrives at Croydon Station, its number will change to Route 668 and continue along the loop. Passengers can travel to all the stops on the loop without changing buses.

Route 669 will travel to Aquinas College in the morning and afternoon on school days. Aquinas College students can catch Route 669 to school instead of Route 380.

Passengers travelling to Maroondah Hospital can catch Route 669.

The frequency and operating hours for routes 668 and 669 will be similar to Route 380.

Passengers should check their timetable and plan ahead.

Route 694 timetable changes

Route 694 Olinda – Belgrave (PDF) 147 KB will have a new timetable.

The timetable change will align with the new opening hours of the SkyHigh restaurant and observatory at Mount Dandenong.

The first two and last two weekday trips will be removed. These services are the:

  • 6:19 am service departing Belgrave Station
  • 6:55 am service departing Belgrave Station
  • 4:48 pm service departing Ridge Rd/Mt Dandenong Tourist Rd (Olinda)
  • 8:01 pm service departing Mount Dandenong Observatory/Observatory Rd (Mount Dandenong).

Temporary route and stop changes around Croydon Station

Due to ongoing works on Devon Street, routes 664, 688, 690, 737, and 967 will have temporary route and stop changes to access the bus interchange.

Some stops along Mt Dandenong Road will be closed until early February 2025. Find out more here.
