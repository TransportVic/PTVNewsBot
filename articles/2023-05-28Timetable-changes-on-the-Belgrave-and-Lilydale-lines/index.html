Timetable changes on the Belgrave and Lilydale lines

From Sunday 28 May 2023, there will be new train timetables for the Belgrave and Lilydale lines. Passengers should check their timetable before traveling.

What’s changing on the Belgrave and Lilydale lines?

  • The new Union Station has opened on the Belgrave and Lilydale lines. Services will now stop at Union Station with an additional three morning and three evening services on weekdays.
  • There will be some minor changes to stopping patterns at Laburnum and Glenferrie stations. Passengers should check the timetable and plan ahead before they travel.
  • Transfer times to weekday Alamein evening services at Camberwell Station will be increased to three minutes, consistent with weekend connection times.

To plan your journey, view the new timetables for the Belgrave Line and Lilydale Line, visit Journey planner, or call 1800 800 007 to order a printed copy of your timetable.
