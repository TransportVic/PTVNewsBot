Ultra Music Festival

Public transport for the Ultra Music Festival at the Flemington Racecourse on Saturday 11 April 2026.

Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner within 14 days of this event, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Metropolitan train

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Trains will operate from Flinders Street (Platforms 9/10) in both directions, stopping at Southern Cross (Platforms 13/14) and North Melbourne (Platforms 5/6), then express to Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1).

Trains will commence from Flinders Street towards Flemington Racecourse at 12:40pm to 5:30pm. There will be a break in service from 5:30pm to 8:30pm. Trains will re-commence from Flemington Racecourse to Flinders Street from 8:30pm. The final train departing Flemington Racecourse towards Flinders Street will be at 12.10am on Sunday 12 April.

Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Extra Trams will operate via Route 57a via Flemington Racecourse, between Stop 01 Flinders Street / Elizabeth Street and Stop 40 Epsom Road/ Maribyrnong Road, for patrons wishing to make their way to and from the event. 

Passengers may alight and board trams at Stop 30 Flemington Racecourse on Epsom Road. 

Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

The following bus route travels close to Flemington Racecourse: 

  • Route 472 (Williamstown to Moonee Ponds via Footscray) (please note service may finish prior to the end of the music festival)

Passengers can catch buses from Footscray Station and alight at Langs Road/Showgrounds.

Regional train

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Passengers arriving at Southern Cross station are advised to change trains and catch a Flemington Racecourse service. Services will return then via Southern Cross Station. 

Refer to the Metropolitan trains section above for service times and platform information.
