Public transport for St Jerome’s Laneway Festival: Friday 14 February 2025

Events > Culture

St Jerome’s Laneway Festival returns to Melbourne at the Flemington Racecourse! Public transport is your best way to enjoy it, with extra services to help you get to and from the Flemington Racecourse on Friday 14 February 2025.

The event will run: 

  • Friday 14 February: 12.30pm to 11pm

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Please visit the event website for further event information here.

 Metropolitan train

Getting to Flemington Racecourse

Trains will operate from Flinders Street (Platform 8/9), stopping at Southern Cross (Platforms 13/14) and North Melbourne (Platforms 5/6), then express to Flemington Racecourse (Platforms 1 / 2). 

  • From 12.10pm, services will depart every 10 minutes.

During the above period, services will return to Flinders Street express with passengers. 

The final service from Flinders Street will depart at 3.30pm.

Leaving from Flemington Racecourse

Train will operate from Flemington Racecourse (Platforms 1/ 2), stopping only at North Melbourne (Platform 5) and Southern Cross (Platform 13), en route to Flinders Street (Platform 10). 

  • From 7.20pm, services will depart Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1) every 20 minutes. 

Between 7.15pm and 8.15pm, services operating from Flinders Street will stop at Southern Cross (Platform 14) and North Melbourne (Platform 6) en route to Flemington Racecourse.

  • From 8.15pm services will operate to Flemington Racecourse express with passengers. 
  • From 10pm, services will depart Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1) every 10 minutes. 
  • From 10.50pm, services will depart from Flemington Racecourse (Platforms 1/2) every 5 to 6 minutes. 

During the above period, services will operate to Flemington Racecourse express with passengers. 

The final service from Flemington Racecourse will depart at 12.10am from (Platform 1).

 Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

The following bus route travels close to Flemington Racecourse: 

  • Route 472 (Williamstown to Moonee Ponds via Footscray)

Passengers can catch buses from Footscray Station and alight at Langs Road/Showgrounds.
