Public transport for Subculture: Saturday 8th June 2024

Events > Culture

Melbourne, prepare yourselves for the next instalment of one of the best trance events in the world.

Extra public transport services are running to and from Melbourne Showgrounds on Saturday 8 June 2024.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Saturday 8 June 2024

 Metropolitan train

Getting to the Melbourne Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Flinders Street Station (Platform 10) stopping at Southern Cross Station (Platform 14), North Melbourne (Platform 6) then Showgrounds (Platform 1).

The first train will depart Flinders Street Station:

  • 11:39am until 12:39pm every 20 minutes
  • 12:59am until 3:30pm every 30 minutes

Leaving the Melbourne Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Melbourne Showgrounds stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 5), Southern Cross Station (Platform 13) and then into Flinders Street (Platform 10).

Trains will depart Showgrounds from:

  • 8:00pm until 9:20pm every 20 minutes
  • 9:30pm until 11:15pm every 15 minutes

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from the Melbourne Showgrounds

Passengers can catch a Route 57 tram, alighting at Stop 32 Sandown Road/Epsom Road.

 Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from the Melbourne Showgrounds

The following bus route travels close to the Racecourse:

  • Route 404 (please note service may finish prior to the end of the music festival)

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

 Regional train

Getting to the Melbourne Showgrounds

Passengers arriving at North Melbourne and Southern Cross stations are advised to change trains and catch a Showgrounds service.

Refer to the Metropolitan trains section above for service times and platform information.

Leaving the Melbourne Showgrounds

Passengers are advised to catch any train from Melbourne Showgrounds and alight at Southern Cross or North Melbourne stations and change to a regional train.
