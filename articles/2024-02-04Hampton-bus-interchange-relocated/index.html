Hampton bus interchange relocated

From Sunday 4 February, the new Hampton bus interchange will open on Koolkuna Lane, as part of the Hampton Station Precinct development.

The new path will see Route 708 Hampton Station to Carrum Station via Southland Shopping Centre and Route 828 Hampton Station to Berwick Station via Dandenong Station make a slight deviation, continuing along Willis Street for a further 200 meters before turning left into Koolkuna Lane, where the new bus interchange is located.

There are no changes to the timetables for Route 708 Hampton Station to Carrum Station via Southland Shopping Centre or Route 828 Hampton Station to Berwick Station via Dandenong Station.
