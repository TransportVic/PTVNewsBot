Public transport for the NRL Round 8: Melbourne Victory v South Sydney Rabbitohs: Friday 25 April 2025

NRL continues at AAMI Park as Melbourne Victory take on South Sydney Rabbitoh’s for the annual ANZAC Day clash.

Public transport is your best way to enjoy it, with extra services running to help you get to and from the AAMI Park on Friday 25 April 2025.

The event will begin at 8:10pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.
