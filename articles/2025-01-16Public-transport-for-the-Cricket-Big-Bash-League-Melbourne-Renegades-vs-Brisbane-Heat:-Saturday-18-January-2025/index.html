Public transport for the Cricket Big Bash League Melbourne Renegades vs Brisbane Heat: Saturday 18 January 2025

Extra services will help you get to and from Marvel Stadium for the Cricket Big Bash League Melbourne Renegades vs Brisbane Heat on Saturday 18 January.

The event will begin at 5pm. 

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Please note: Buses will replace trains between Reservoir and Mernda

On the return journey, Mernda passengers are advised to catch a Reservoir services from Southern Cross (Platform 9) and then change at Reservoir for a replacement bus.
