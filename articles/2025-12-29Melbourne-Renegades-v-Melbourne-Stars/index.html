Melbourne Renegades v Melbourne Stars

The Big Bash League is back for another season – bringing all the colour and excitement to Marvel Stadium. Extra services will help you get to and from the match on Saturday 10 January.

The match will begin at 7:15 pm and concludes at approximately 22:25 pm.

Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from Marvel Stadium

Extra trams will operate as Route 30a via La Trobe Street, and as Route 86a via Bourke Street.

Take advantage of the city’s Free Tram Zone which extends from Queen Victoria Market to Docklands, Spring Street, Flinders Street Station and Federation Square.
