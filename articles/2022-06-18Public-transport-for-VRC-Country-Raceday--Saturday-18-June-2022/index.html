Public transport for VRC Country Raceday: Saturday 18 June 2022

Extra public transport services are running to and from VRC Country Raceday at Flemington Racecourse on Saturday 18 June 2022.

Gates open: 10.50am

First race: 11.50am

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Before you travel

QR codes have been rolled out across the public transport network to help keep passengers safe. Please check-in every time you see a QR code and register your myki as well. The details you share are used to contact you when necessary, to prevent further spread of COVID-19 in the community.

When travelling on public transport you must wear a fitted face mask if you are eight years and over covering your nose and mouth, unless you have a lawful reason for not doing so, and ensure you wear it for your entire journey.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Metropolitan train

Getting to the Racecourse

Train services will operate from Southern Cross Station (Platform 8) to Flemington Racecourse stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 2) and then express to Flemington Racecourse Station (Platform 1).

Trains depart Flinders Street Station from:

  • 10.30am until 12.50pm every 20 minutes

Leaving the Racecourse

Train services will operate from Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1) to Southern Cross Station (Platform 8), stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 1).

Trains to depart from:

  • 3.50pm until 4:27pm every 40 minutes
  • 5:07pm until 6.10pm every 20 minutes

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

Getting to and from the Racecourse

Passengers can catch a Route 57 tram, alighting at Stop 29 Racecourse Road/Flemington Racecourse.
