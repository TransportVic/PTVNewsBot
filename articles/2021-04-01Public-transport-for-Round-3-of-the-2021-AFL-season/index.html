Before you travel

We encourage you to register your myki so we can contact you if you've travelled on a public transport service identified as a COVID-19 site. Register your myki now - it's quick and easy.

You must wear a fitted face mask covering your nose and mouth on public transport and ensure you wear it for your entire journey. It's also strongly recommended you wear a face mask when you cannot maintain 1.5 metres distance from other people, such as at busy stations and stops.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Disruptions

Footy fans please note the following disruptions may affect your travel to and from the game. Allow extra travel time and plan ahead using Journey planner.

Disruptions between Thursday 1 April and Monday 5 April

  • Ballarat Line: Coaches replace evening trains
  • Bendigo, Echuca and Swan Hill lines: Coaches replace trains
  • Geelong Line: Coaches replace evening trains
  • Hurstbridge and Mernda Lines: Buses replace evening trains
  • Sunbury line: Buses replace trains
  • Weribee and Williamstown lines: Buses replace trains

Visiting the MCG and Marvel Stadium

  • Make sure you download your mobile ticket before you arrive at the venue and scan in using your smartphone.
  • Download the Service Victoria app to ensure you're ready to scan the displayed QR codes on arrival. The Service Victoria App can be downloaded on Google Play or the App Store.
