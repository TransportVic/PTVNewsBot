Public transport for the ANZAC Day Shrine Dawn Service: Tuesday 25 April 2023

The 2023 Shrine Dawn Service will be held from 6am to 6.30am on Tuesday 25 April at the Shrine of Remembrance. 

Some additional services will operate; however, passengers are advised to allow additional time to get to the Shrine of Remembrance. 

Some customers will be eligible for free transport on ANZAC Day. For more information, see ANZAC Day 2023.

 Metropolitan train

To supplement regular services, over 15 additional services will operate to assist customers in attending the Shrine Dawn Service, with all services arriving at Flinders Street Station by 5.30am.

Detailed disruption information will be provided closer to the day.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.

 Metropolitan tram

Additional trams will operate on all routes except for Routes 78, 79, 82 and City Circle trams (Route 35) to assist customers attending the Dawn Service and arrive in the city by 5.30am.

Swanston Street Shuttles will operate on Swanston St/St Kilda Rd corridor from Flinders Lane towards Stop 20 ANZAC Station running every 3 minutes (approx.) until 5.32am.
