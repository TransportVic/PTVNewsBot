Public transport for the BBL match Melbourne Stars v Hobart Hurricanes: Friday 16 December 2022

It's time for a game of BBL cricket! Public transport is your best way to enjoy it, as the Melbourne Stars face off against Hobart Hurricanes. Extra services will help you get to and from the MCG on Friday 16 December.

The event will begin at 5:05pm.
