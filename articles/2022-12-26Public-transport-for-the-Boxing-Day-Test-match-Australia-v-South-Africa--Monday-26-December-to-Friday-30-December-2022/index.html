Public transport for the Boxing Day Test match Australia v South Africa: Monday 26 December to Friday 30 December 2022

Cricket is back and now it time to enjoy of the Boxing Day Test, and for the first time in 14 years Australia will host South Africa. This is an iconic day in the sporting calendar and not to be missed and Public Transport is assisting everybody with the experience of Test Match cricket with extra services will help you get to and from the MCG.

Match day starts at 10:30am

  • Travelling on Monday 26 December
  • Travelling on Tuesday 27 December
  • Travelling on Wednesday 28, Thursday 29 and Friday 30 December

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Gate guide for the MCG

Please refer to the gate guide below to see which mode of transport will get you closest to your gate.

Gates 1 - 4

  • Jolimont Station (train)
  • Stop 11 on Wellington Parade (tram Route 48)

Gates 1, 2 and 7

  • Flinders Street Station (train) and walk through Birrarung Marr

Gates 1, 2, 6 and 7

  • Richmond Station - Swan Street exit (train), 7 minute walk
  • Stops 7C and 7D (Route 70/70a trams)

Gates 3 - 5

  • Richmond Station - Brunton Avenue exit (train), 5 minute walk
  • Stop Rowena Parade/Punt Road (bus route 246)
