Changes to Melbourne metropolitan bus services from Sunday 11 April

From Sunday 11 April 2021 changes will be made to the timetables of many bus routes in metropolitan Melbourne. These changes complete the re-coordination of approximately 360 bus and coach routes with Victoria’s new train timetables.

You can now access the new coach and bus timetables for the re-coordinated services on our Timetables page, order a printed copy of your timetable by calling 1800 800 007 or use the Journey planner to plan ahead.

Changes to train timetables from Sunday 31 January could affect your connecting bus journey. Please plan ahead before you travel using our Journey planner. You can learn about bus routes that have been re-coordinated with the new train timetable earlier this year at:
