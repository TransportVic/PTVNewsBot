2025 Formula 1 Australian Grand Prix

Free trams to the Grand Prix

If you're going to the Grand Prix this weekend, the best way to get there is on one of the many free trams.

There are dedicated express trams for Grand Prix ticketholders running from Southern Cross Station, Melbourne Central and Flinders Street (St Kilda Road). The trams will run from 5.30am until 10pm each day.

Southern Cross Station trams will run to Gates 1, 2 and 3. Flinders Street Station and Melbourne Central trams will run to Gates 5, 8, 9 and 10.

Regular bus services will also run to the event including on surrounding routes 246, 600, 604, 606, 922, 923.

Rideshare and taxi drop off and pick up points will be available on Canterbury Road, Albert Road, St Kilda Road and Fitzroy Street.

To better support the movement of people to and from the Grand Prix and vicinity, buses will replace trams on some nearby routes and there are other tram service changes across Melbourne.

We encourage you to plan your trip with our Grand Prix journey planner.

Road closures

Albert Park closures and changes

Lakeside Drive and roads within Albert Park are closed to the public.

Changes on Cantebury Road:

  • single land closure southbound
  • median openings closed
  • turns banned
  • speed reduction to 40km/h 


Changes to Albert Road:

  • speed reduction to 40km/h


Note: There are changed parking restrictions including temporary tow away zones on local and arterial roads surrounding the venue.

Melbourne CBD closures

Road closures will be in place at various times from Thursday to Sunday on:

  • Spencer Street
  • Bourke Street
  • St Kilda Road

This is to ensure safety for pedestrians and trams. If you're driving in the city on the weekend, please take extra care.
