Melbourne Stars v Adelaide Strikers

The Big Bash League is back for another season – bringing all the colour and excitement to the ‘G. Extra services will help you get to and from the MCG on Tuesday 13 January.
The match will begin at 7:15 pm and concludes at approximately 10:25 pm.
 

Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner within 14 days of this event, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Metropolitan train

Metro Trains Additional services

Additional services for the following lines depart from Jolimont and Richmond stations after the game:

  • Clifton Hill, Greensborough, Flinders Street, Ringwood, Glen Waverley, and Cheltenham

Mernda Passengers are to alight on Clifton Hill/Greensborough services from Jolimont (Platform 2), then transfer at Clifton Hill for a bus connection.

Pakenham and Cranbourne Passengers are to alight on Cheltenham services from Richmond (Platform 4), then transfer at Caulfield for a bus connection.

Sandringham Passengers are to alight on Frankston services from Richmond (Platform 4), then transfer at South Yarra for a bus connection.

Werribee, Williamstown and Sunbury Passengers are to alight on any available service from Richmond (Platforms 3, 7 & 8) or Jolimont (Platform 1), then transfer at Flinders Street for a Northern Group service to North Melbourne.
