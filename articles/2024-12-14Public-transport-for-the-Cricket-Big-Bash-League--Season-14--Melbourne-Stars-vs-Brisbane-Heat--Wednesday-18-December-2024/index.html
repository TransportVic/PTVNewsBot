Public transport for the Cricket Big Bash League (Season 14) Melbourne Stars vs Brisbane Heat: Wednesday 18 December 2024

Events > Sport

The Big Bash League is back for another season bringing all the colour and excitement to the MCG. There are some amazing fixtures, including Melbourne Stars vs Brisbane Heat on Wednesday 18th December – perfect for a Christmas Party Bash in style! Public transport is your best way to enjoy this, as the Melbourne Stars take on Brisbane Heat. Extra services will help you get to and from the MCG on Wednesday 18 December. 

The event will begin at 7.15pm.
