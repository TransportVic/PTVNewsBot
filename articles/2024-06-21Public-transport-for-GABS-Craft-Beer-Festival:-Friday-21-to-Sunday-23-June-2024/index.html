Public transport for GABS Craft Beer Festival: Friday 21 to Sunday 23 June 2024

Events > Culture

Melbourne, discover over 500 beer taps, cocktails, whisky, wines and more when you make your way to Melbourne Showgrounds for the GABS Craft Beer Festival. Meet and share stories with a cracking line up of some of the best brewers. Enjoy Live Entertainment, silent disco, masterclasses, street food, fun games and so much more!

Extra public transport services are running to and from Melbourne Showgrounds from Friday 21st to Sunday 23rd June 2024.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Friday 21st June 2024

 Metropolitan train

Session 01

Getting to the Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Flinders Street Station (Platform 9) stopping at Southern Cross Station (Platform 14), North Melbourne (Platform 6) then Showgrounds (Platform 1).

Train will depart Flinders Street Station at:

  • 10:31am, 10:39am, 10:53am and 10:58am

Leaving the Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Melbourne Showgrounds stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 5), Southern Cross Station (Platform 13) and then into Flinders Street (Platform 9 & 10).

Trains will depart Showgrounds from:

  • 4:35pm until 6:18pm every 10 to 15 minutes

Session 02

Getting to the Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Flinders Street Station (Platform 9) stopping at Southern Cross Station (Platform 14), North Melbourne (Platform 6) then Showgrounds (Platform 1).

Trains will depart Flinders Street Station at:

  • 5:15pm, 5:25pm, 5:45pm and 6pm

Leaving the Showgrounds

Train services will operate from Melbourne Showgrounds stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 5), Southern Cross Station (Platform 13) and then into Flinders Street (Platform 9 & 10).

Trains will depart Showgrounds from:

  • 11:35pm until 12:15am every 10 minutes
