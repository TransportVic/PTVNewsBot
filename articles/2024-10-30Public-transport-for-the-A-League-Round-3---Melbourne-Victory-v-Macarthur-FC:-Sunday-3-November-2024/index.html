Public transport for the A-League Round 3 - Melbourne Victory v Macarthur FC: Sunday 3 November 2024

A-League is back at AAMI Park. This matchup is sure to deliver edge-of-your-seat action. Public transport is your best way to enjoy it, as Melbourne Victory FC takes on the Macarthur FC. Extra services will help you get to and from AAMI Park on Sunday 3 November. 

The event will begin at 4pm.
