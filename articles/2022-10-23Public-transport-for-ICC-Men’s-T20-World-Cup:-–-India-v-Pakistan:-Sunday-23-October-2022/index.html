Public transport for ICC Men’s T20 World Cup: – India v Pakistan: Sunday 23 October 2022

ICC Men’s T20 World Cup games is back in Melbourne and returning to the G! Public transport is your best way to enjoy it, as India faces off against Pakistan. Extra services will help you get to and from the MCG on Sunday 23 October 2022.

Fan Zone opens at 3pm in Yarra Park
MCG Gates open at 4:30pm
Match Starts at 7pm
