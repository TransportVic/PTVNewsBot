Melbourne Victory v Central Coast Mariners

Experience the thrill of the A-League Men 2025-26 season as Melbourne Victory take on Coast Mariners at AAMI Park.

The game will begin at 5:00pm

Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Metropolitan train

Additional services for the following lines depart after the match:

  • Ringwood, Glen Waverly, Frankston, Sandringham, Glen Waverly, Frankston, Sandringham

Mernda / Hurstbridge passengers are to board a Frankston service at Richmond (Platform 3) and transfer at Parliament for a bus. 

Pakenham / Cranbourne Passengers are to board a Westall service from Town Hall, then transfer at Westall for a bus. 

Passengers wanting to alight on services departing Flinders Street are advised to alight on trains departing from Richmond or Jolimont or alternatively board Route 75 Trams.
