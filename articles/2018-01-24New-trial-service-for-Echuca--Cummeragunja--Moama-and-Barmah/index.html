New trial service for Echuca, Cummeragunja, Moama and Barmah

From Thursday 24 January 2018 there'll be a new trial service between Echuca and Barmah, servicing the communities of Cummeragunja and Moama.

The trial service will operate every second Thursday, providing morning and afternoon return trips. View a route map and timetable or plan your journey.

For further information or to request a printed timetable, call 1800 800 007.
