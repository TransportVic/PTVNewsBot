Public transport for the Victorian Caravan, Camping and Touring Supershow: Wednesday 22 February to Sunday 26 February 2023

Victorian Caravan, Camping & Touring Supershow is returning to Melbourne Showground and Public Transport is the best way to travel to and from Victorian Caravan, Camping & Touring Supershow, with extra trains running to and from Melbourne Showgrounds from Friday 24 February to Sunday 26 February only

Please also check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey before you travel.

Wednesday 22 February and Thursday 23 February

Passengers are advised to catch a Craigieburn line train, change at Newmarket Station and then catch a Route 57 tram to Melbourne Showgrounds.

Friday 24 February

 Metropolitan train

Getting to the Showgrounds

Trains will depart from Flinders Street Station (Platforms 7/8/9), stopping at Southern Cross Station (Platform 14), North Melbourne Station (Platform 6) and then express to Showgrounds Station from:

  • 9:16am to 3:14pm every 20 minutes

Leaving the Showgrounds

Trains will depart the Showgrounds from:

  • 9:35am to 3:37pm every 20 minutes

The last train departs Showgrounds Station at 3.37pm.

Passengers travelling outside these times are advised to catch a Craigieburn line train, change at Newmarket Station and then catch a Route 57 tram to Melbourne Showgrounds.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from the Showgrounds

Passengers can catch a Route 57 tram, alighting at Stop 32 Sandown Road. 

Passenger advise to walk approximately 200 metres to Showgrounds entrance.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.

Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from the Showgrounds

The following bus route travels close to the Showgrounds:

  • Route 472 (Williamstown - Moonee Ponds via Footscray) - Alight at stop RAS Showgrounds/Langs Road (Ascot Vale).

Plan your journey using Journey Planner.
