V/Line Industrial Action Withdrawn – Friday 22 March and Thursday 28 March

Travelling in Victoria > Regional Victoria

The Rail, Tram and Bus Union has informed V/Line of its agreement to withdraw industrial action planned for Friday 22 March and Thursday 28 March.

V/Line trains will now be running on both these days.

Coaches will continue to replace trains on the Seymour and Shepparton lines on due to planned track works.

Please check PTV or the V/Line website or mobile app before travelling for the latest information.

Thank you for your patience during this time.
