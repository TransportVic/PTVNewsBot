Public transport for the NRL Round 2 – Melbourne Storm v New Zealand Warriors: Saturday 16 March 2024

NRL is back at AAMI Park. This matchup is sure to deliver edge-of-your-seat action. Public transport is your best way to enjoy it, as the Melbourne Storm takes on the New Zealand Warriors. Extra services will help you get to and from AAMI Park on Saturday 16 March.

The event will begin at 7:35pm.
