Public transport for the BBL T20 - Melbourne Renegades v Perth Scorchers: Wednesday 22 December 2021

It's time for the BBL League to return to Marvel Stadium. Public Transport is your best way to enjoy it, as the Melbourne Renegades go into battle with the Perth Scorchers. Extra services will help you get home from Marvel Stadium on Wednesday 22 December 2021.

The event will begin at 7.15pm.

Before you travel

QR codes have been rolled out across the public transport network to help keep passengers safe. Please check-in every time you see a QR code and register your myki as well. The details you share are used to contact you when necessary, to prevent further spread of COVID-19 in the community

When travelling on public transport you must wear a fitted face mask covering your nose and mouth, unless you have a lawful reason for not doing so, and ensure you wear it for your entire journey.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.
