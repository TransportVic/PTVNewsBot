Public transport for Turnbull Stakes Day

Events > Racing

We're running extra services for the VRC Turnbull Stakes Raceday on Saturday 5 October.

Gates open: 11am 

First race: 12:25pm 

Gates close: 5:40pm 

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

 Metropolitan train

To the Racecourse

Catch a train to Flemington Racecourse from Flinders Street (Platform 9), Southern Cross Station (Platform 14) or North Melbourne Station (Platform 6).

Trains leave:

  • Every 20 minutes from 10:40am to 1:31pm.

From the Racecourse

Catch any train from Flemington Racecourse to Southern Cross or North Melbourne stations.

  • Every 20 minutes from 1:50pm until 6:46pm

Last train leaves at 6:46pm.

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Extra Route 57 trams run to and from Stop 29 Racecourse Road/Flemington Racecourse.

 Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Catch a Route 404 bus to and from the Racecourse. The last service may run before the event ends.

 Regional train

Getting to Flemington Racecourse

Catch a train from North Melbourne or Southern Cross stations to Flemington Racecourse.

From Flemington Racecourse

Catch a train from Flemington Racecourse to North Melbourne or Southern Cross stations for regional trains.
