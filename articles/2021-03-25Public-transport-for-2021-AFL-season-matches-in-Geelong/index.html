Public transport for 2021 AFL season matches in Geelong

Public transport is the best way to get to the game during the 2021 AFL season!

AFL matches in Geelong

Extra V/Line train services run to and from matches in Geelong. Plan your trip using Journey planner.

Port Phillip Ferries offers a ferry service from Docklands to Geelong for these games:

  • St Kilda v West Coast Eagles, GMHBA Stadium - Saturday 24 April at 1.45pm

Dates and times for AFL games in rounds 7 to 23 will be confirmed at a later date.

Ferry services also run from Geelong to Docklands for games at the MCG and Marvel Stadium.

For information on the timetable, fares and to book your ticket, visit the Port Phillip Ferries website.

Before you travel

We encourage you to register your myki so we can contact you if you've travelled on a public transport service identified as a COVID-19 site. Register your myki now - it's quick and easy.

You must wear a fitted face mask covering your nose and mouth on public transport and at indoor stations and stops and ensure you wear it for your entire journey.

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.
