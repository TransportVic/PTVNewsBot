New bus timetables to better connect with Ballarat Line trains

From 13 April 2025, most weekend bus services that connect to trains on the Ballarat Line will have new timetables and now run every 40 minutes.

The new bus timetables provide better connections with the new train timetable on the Ballarat Line.

Timetables and more information will be available on this page from mid-March 2025.
