Hypersonic Music Festival

Public transport for the Hypersonic music festival at the Flemington Racecourse on Sunday 30 November 2025. 

The event will run from 12pm to 10pm.

Before you travel

If you use Journey Planner, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Metropolitan train

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Trains will operate from Flinders Street (Platform 10), stopping at Southern Cross (Platforms 13/14) and North Melbourne (Platforms 5/6) in both directions, then express to Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1).

Metropolitan tram

Regular Trams will operate via Route 57 between Stop 01 Flinders Street / Elizabeth Street and Stop 30: Flemington Drive/ Epsom Road, for patrons wishing to make their way to and from the event. 

Please note that due to the planned disruption on Route 57, buses will replace trams between Stop 31 Racing Victoria/ Epsom Road and Stop 49 Central Park Avenue/Cordite Avenue and divert around the road closure at Epsom Road and Union Road.

Passengers travelling to and from the City are advised to board and alight from Stop 30.

Passengers travelling to and from the Suburbs are advised to board a replacement bus shuttle from Stop 31 Racing Victoria/ Epsom Road.

For further information on Planned Disruptions please visit our website here. 

Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

The following bus route travels close to Flemington Racecourse: 

  • Route 472 (Williamstown to Moonee Ponds via Footscray) (please note service may finish prior to the end of the music festival)

Passengers can catch buses from Footscray Station and alight at Langs Road/Showgrounds. 

Regional train

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Passengers arriving at Southern Cross station are advised to change trains and catch a Flemington Racecourse service. Services will return then via Southern Cross Station.

Refer to the Metropolitan trains section above for platform information.
