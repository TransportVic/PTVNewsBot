Try Before You Ride

Inclusive transport for all Victorians

These events are for everyone, including those with accessibility needs, parents with kids, and older people. 

Our friendly staff will be available to guide you throughout the day and answer your questions about public transport. Don't miss out on this unique opportunity to try before you ride!

Event Details

When: Thursday 16 October 2025
Where: Southern Cross Station
Address:  Spencer Street Docklands, VIC 3008
Time: 10:30am - 2pm
Cost: Free
Bookings: Register on Eventbrite to reserve your spot.

What to expect on the day

  • Practice getting on and off our trains, trams, buses, and accessible taxis.
  • Learn about and familiarise yourself with accessibility features on public transport.
  • Speak with public transport operators and staff.
  • Go on a familiarisation tour of Southern Cross Station (register on the day)
  • Take a tram ride to discover what travelling by tram is like.

Accessibility services available at the event

Buggy service to move around the event space

  • Personal care
  • Quiet space

These services are provided by Travellers Aid. Use the link below to view more information about Travellers Aid at Southern Cross Station.

This event is coordinated by Yarra Trams, Metro Trains, V/Line, Kinetic Buses, and Travellers Aid. It is organised in partnership with Transport Victoria, Safe Transport Victoria, and Southern Cross Station.

Intepreter services

An Auslan intepreter is available on request. Please request this service when registering for the event. Requests are subject to availability.
