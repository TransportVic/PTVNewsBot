Circoloco

Public transport for Circoloco at the Flemington Racecourse on Saturday 24 January 2026. The event will run from 1pm to 11pm. 

Public Transport is free statewide on weekends in January. 

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey. 

If you use Journey Planner within 14 days of this event, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.

Metropolitan train

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Trains will run every 10 – 20 minutes between Flinders Street, Southern Cross, North Melbourne, and Flemington Racecourse Stations. There will be a break in services, with the last train departing Flinders Street towards Flemington Racecourse at 4:44pm. Trains will recommence from Flemington Racecourse towards Flinders Street from 8pm. 

Trains are also running to support an event at Footscray Park. 

Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Extra Trams will operate via Route 57a between Stop 01 Flinders Street / Elizabeth Street and Stop 30 Flemington Drive/ Epsom Road, these trams run along Flemington Road, close to Parkville Station. 

Ordinary route 57 trams continue to service North Melbourne and Maribyrnong. 

Unfortunately, all trams to and from Flemington Racecourse have steps. Consider trains and buses if stepless travel is required. 

Metropolitan bus

Getting to Flemington Racecourse

The following bus route travels close to Flemington Racecourse, refer to timetables to see when they run. Unfortunately, these routes conclude prior to the event finishing. 

  • Route 472 (Williamstown to Moonee Ponds via Footscray) Flemington Racecourse/Fischer Parade
  • Route 404 (Footscray to Moonee Ponds via Newmarket) Epsom Rd/Ascot Vale Rd. 

Regional train

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Change trains at Southern Cross for trains to/from Flemington Racecourse.
