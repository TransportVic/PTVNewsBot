Public transport for AFLW Grand Final: North Melbourne v Brisbane

The AFLW Grand Final is on in Melbourne at IKON Park and public transport is your best way to enjoy it! We're running extra services to help you get home from IKON Park on Saturday 30 November 2024.

Event Start: 7:45pm

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

 Metropolitan train

Normal train services run to and from Royal Park on the Upfield Line. Catch a train to Royal Park and walk approximately 15 minutes to Ikon Park.

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from IKON Park

Extra Route 19/19a trams run between Stop 19 Brunswick Road and Stop 5 Melbourne Central Station/La Trobe Street.

 Metropolitan bus

Customers can catch a Route 505 bus close to IKON Park.

The last bus may run before the event ends.

 Regional train

Normal services run to and from Southern Cross Station. You can catch a Route 19/19a tram via Flinders Street or a metropolitan train to and from Royal Park Station.
