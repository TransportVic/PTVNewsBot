More services for routes 733 and 767

From 30 April 2023, more services will be introduced on routes 733 Oakleigh – Box Hill via Clayton & Monash University & Mt Waverley and 767 Southland – Box Hill via Chadstone & Jordanville & Deakin University.

Passengers will benefit from an extra 154 weekly trips on Route 733 and an extra 71 weekly trips on Route 767.

Route 733 will run every 15 minutes during the middle of the day between Box Hill and Monash University and will run every 40 minutes on Sundays.

Route 767 will run every 20 minutes during the middle of the day.

Passengers should check their timetable before they travel.

To plan your journey, view the new timetables for routes 733 and 767, visit Journey planner, or call 1800 800 007 to order a printed copy of your timetable.
