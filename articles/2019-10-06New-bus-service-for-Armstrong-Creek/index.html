New bus service for Armstrong Creek

The new Route 45 bus service will connect the fast-growing area of Armstrong Creek to key destinations. Services start from Sunday 6 October 2019.

It will give the community public transport access to key destinations including:

  • Warralily Village shopping centre
  • Waurn Ponds shopping centre
  • Waurn Ponds Station.

The service will run seven days a week, every 30 to 50 minutes in peak periods and around every 80 minutes at other times on weekdays, and around every 80 minutes on weekends.

Slight timing variations do occur. For full details, please check the timetable.

To plan your journey, visit Journey planner. To order a printed copy of a timetable, call 1800 800 007.
