Get to the races in style

A world of excitement awaits as the Melbourne Cup Carnival returns to Flemington Racecourse from Saturday 2 November until Saturday 9 November 2024.

Public Transport is your best option with extra services running to get to and from the races at Flemington.

Before you travel

Before you travel 

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Make your journey hassle-free and remember to top up your myki and have it ready to use before travelling.
