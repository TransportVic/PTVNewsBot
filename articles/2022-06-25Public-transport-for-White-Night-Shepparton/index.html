Before you travel

Please continue to check this page for more details and up-to-date information before you travel.

When travelling on public transport you must wear a fitted face mask covering your nose and mouth, unless you have a lawful reason for not doing so, and ensure you wear it for your entire journey.

Getting to and from Shepparton

Regional train (V/Line)

To Shepparton

Normal timetable services will operate from Southern Cross Station.

From Shepparton

Normal timetable services will operate from Shepparton Station.

Regional coach (V/Line)

There are no additional coaches operating from Shepparton to Melbourne.

Getting around Shepparton

Regional bus

Buses in Shepparton will run to their normal timetable until 4pm, however from 12pm there will be road closures implemented around the Shepparton CBD for White Night.

Route 07 Marketplace will not operate from 3:50pm.

Route 09 Aquamoves will not operate from 4pm.

From 5:30pm to 12:30am, seven bus routes will operate every thirty minutes to and from the Shepparton town centre. These routes run to and from the bus interchange on Maude Street. 

Buses will pick up and drop off at passengers at bus stops along these routes. Passengers are to hail driver to board/disembark bus.

Route 1 – Parkside Gardens via GV Health

Route 2 – Parkside Gardens via The Boulevard

Route 3 – Golf Drive/TAFE

Route 4 – Connolly Park

Route 5 - Archer

Route 6 - South East

Route 8 – Kialla

Buses will run every 30 minutes from 5:30pm to 12:30am, and passengers will be required to pay to travel onboard.

More details on bus stop locations and service times will be available soon.

Park and Ride

From 5:30pm to 12:30am, there will be two Park and Rides where you can park your car and then catch an express bus to the Maude Street bus interchange for free.

Buses will depart every 15 minutes from the Park and Ride locations at:

  • Mooroopna Recreation Reserve, Mooroopna
  • Marketplace, Shepparton
