Changes to Bendigo – Shepparton via Kyabram route

What’s changing for the Bendigo – Shepparton via Kyabram route?

  • From Sunday 29 January 2023, the Bendigo – Shepparton via Kyabram route will have a new timetable.
  • The new timetable will provide a reduced journey time for passengers.
  • There will be a minor route change as the service will make an additional stop at the Shepparton Bus Interchange on Maude Street.

Passengers should check their timetable before they travel.

To plan your journey, view the new timetable for Bendigo – Shepparton via Kyabram, visit Journey planner or call 1800 800 007 to order a printed copy of your timetable.
