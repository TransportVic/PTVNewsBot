Minor timetable adjustments for Swan Hill and Mildura V/Line coaches

From Sunday 11 July 2021, minor timetable adjustments are being made to selected V/Line coach routes at Swan Hill and Mildura.

  • Mildura to Swan Hill - from Monday to Saturday the current 10.15am service will depart 15 minutes earlier for an increased connection time with the train service at Swan Hill.
  • Swan Hill to Mildura - the current 1.15pm Monday to Sunday service will depart Swan Hill Station 13 minutes earlier. This adjustment will provide a shorter connection time between trains and buses and will get passengers home earlier.
  • Mildura to Bendigo - from Monday to Friday the current 7.29am service will depart 15 minutes earlier for a reliable connection with the Bendigo to Southern Cross Station train service.

To plan your journey, visit the Journey planner or order a printed copy of a timetable by calling 1800 800 007.
