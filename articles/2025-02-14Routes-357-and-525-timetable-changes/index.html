Routes 357 and 525 timetable changes

From 14 February 2025, three Route 357 Friday and Saturday Night Buses from Epping Station will leave slightly later to improve connections with the late-night train services:

  • 10:52pm bus will leave at 10:54pm
  • 11:52pm bus will leave at 11:54pm
  • 12:42am bus will leave at 12:54am

From 17 February 2025, two Route 525 buses will leave from Craigieburn Station slightly earlier to improve connections to trains at Donnybrook Station:

  • The 6.14am weekday bus will leave at 6.05am
  • The 6.50am weekday bus will leave at 6.45am

The changes will make it easier and safer for passengers to connect between buses and trains.

To plan your journey:

  • Download the PTV app
  • Use the journey planner
  • Check the Route 357 timetable , the Route 525 timetable
  • or call 1800 800 007.
