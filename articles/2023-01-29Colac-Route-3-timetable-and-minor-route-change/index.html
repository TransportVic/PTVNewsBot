Colac Route 3 timetable and minor route change

From Sunday 29 January 2023, Colac Route 3 will have a timetable and minor route change.

Due to the closure of the Colanda Residential Services in early 2022, the Tuesday diversion to this stop will be permanently removed.

The Tuesday timetable will change to the current Monday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday timetable.

Passengers should check their timetable before they travel.
