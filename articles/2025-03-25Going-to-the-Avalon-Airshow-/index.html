Going to the Avalon Airshow?

Extra services to get you to the airfield and back

Large crowds are set to descend on Avalon Airport for the Avalon Australian International Airshow 2025.  

We’re running extra services from Friday to Sunday to get you there and back . Regional fare cap makes travelling from Melbourne  the quickest and most affordable way to get there. Fares are capped at $11 on weekdays, $7.60 on weekends and $3.80 for concession holders.

Dedicated shuttle buses will run between Lara Station and the Airshow connecting with extra train services.

Driving to the Airshow

Long queues are expected on the Princes Freeway and roads surrounding Avalon Airport. If you must drive, be prepared for long delays.

The Avalon Airshow will be running Park and Ride services for the first time to get people to and from the main gate.

There is no free parking. Parking passes must be pre-purchased ($35 to $65). Please follow the instructions on your ticket instead of using navigation apps to reach the carpark.

If you're driving between Melbourne and Geelong please allow extra time for your journey, as delays are expected on the freeway and roads near the event. Consider catching the train.
