Lara town bus timetable changes

From Sunday 31 March 2019, there'll be minor changes to the Geelong Line train timetable. To keep connections between buses and trains, the following buses in Lara will depart one minute earlier:

  • Lara Route 10 Lara Station to Lara South and Corio Shopping Centre (Monday to Friday) will depart Skylark Circuit/Forest Road South at 8.05am instead of 8.06am and arrive at Lara Station at 8.15am
  • Lara Route 12 Lara Station to Lara West (Monday to Friday) will depart Eastlakes Boulevard/Canterbury Road at 7.59am instead of 8am and arrive at Lara Station at 8.15am

New bus and train timetables will be available from Friday 1 March on our website and at staffed train stations.
