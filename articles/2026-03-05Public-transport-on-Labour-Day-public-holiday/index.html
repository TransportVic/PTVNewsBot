Public transport on Labour Day public holiday

On public holidays, daily myki fare caps apply ($8 full fare and $4 concession/senior), giving you unlimited travel for the day.

For more ticketing information, including myki, regional travel and fare caps, visit: Tickets and myki – Transport Victoria.

Metropolitan train

  • Metro trains will run to a Saturday timetable.
  • Night Network services will not run.

Metropolitan tram

  • Trams run to a Saturday timetable.
  • Services will finish as per the normal Monday timetable.
  • Night network services will not run.

Regional train and coach 

V/Line services will run to a Saturday timetable for the Labour Day public holiday on Monday 9 March, except:

  • Swan Hill Line trains will run to a Sunday timetable.

Bendigo Line

  • The 3:47pm Eaglehawk to Southern Cross train will depart from Eaglehawk later at 6.45pm. 
  • This is due to the service originating at Swan Hill and running to a Sunday timetable.

Night Coaches

  • All night coaches will not run.

Metropolitan and regional bus

  • Metropolitan buses will mostly run to a Saturday timetable
  • Regional bus timetables will vary by operator.
  • Night network services will not run.

Plan your journey

  • Plan ahead by using the Journey planner or by downloading the free PTV app for real-time travel information.
