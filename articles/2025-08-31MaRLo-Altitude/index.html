MaRLo Altitude

MaRLo Altitude is returning to Melbourne, taking place at Flemington Racecourse. The event features multiple sets by the renowned artist MaRLo and will include his signature “Tech Energy” and “V3NOM” sets.

The event will run:

  • 4pm - 11pm

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey. 

Please visit the event website for further event information here.

Metropolitan train

Getting to Flemington Racecourse

Trains will operate from Flinders Street (Platform 10), stopping at Southern Cross (Platforms 13/14) and North Melbourne (Platforms 5/6), then express to Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1).

  • From 3.40pm, services will depart every 20 minutes.

  • From 5.40pm, services will depart every 30 minutes.

The final service from Flinders Street will depart at 7.40pm. During the above period, services will return to Flinders Street with passengers, via North Melbourne and Southern Cross.

Leaving from Flemington Racecourse

Trains will operate from Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1), via North Melbourne and Southern Cross, then express to Flinders Street (Platform 10).

  • From 9.30pm, services will depart every 30 minutes

  • From 11pm, services will depart every 10 minutes

The final service from Flemington Racecourse will depart at 11.50pm.

Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from Melbourne Showgrounds via Route 57

  • Additional trams will operate via Route 57a from Stop 01 Flinders Street / Elizabeth Street and Stop 32 Sandown Road/ Epsom Road via Flemington Road.

Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse. 

The following bus route travels close to Flemington Racecourse: 

  • Route 472 (Williamstown to Moonee Ponds via Footscray)

Passengers can catch buses from Footscray Station and alight at Langs Road/Showgrounds.
