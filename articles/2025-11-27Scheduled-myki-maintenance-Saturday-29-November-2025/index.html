Scheduled myki maintenance Saturday 29 November 2025

We’re making planned myki system upgrades on Saturday 29 November between 12:30am and 4:00am.

During the upgrade, you’ll be unable to access your myki account, top up online or top up instantly via the PTV app.

During the upgrade, you can top up your myki card at:

  • at authorised retail outlets, including all 7-Elevens within the myki boundary
  • staffed train stations
  • myki machinesat selected stations and stops.


You’ll be able to access your myki account and top up once the upgrade is complete.

Thank you for your patience while we complete these upgrades.
