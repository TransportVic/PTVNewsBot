Public transport for the A-League Round 27 – Melbourne Victory v Auckland FC

Join Melbourne Victory at AAMI Park as they take on Auckland FC in their second-to-last home game of the 2025 A-League season.

Public transport is your best way to enjoy the action and we're running extra services to help you get home from AAMI Park on Saturday 19 April.

Kick-off is at 5pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.
