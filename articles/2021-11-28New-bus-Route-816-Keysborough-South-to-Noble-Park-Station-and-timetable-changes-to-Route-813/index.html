New bus Route 816 Keysborough South to Noble Park Station and timetable changes to Route 813

From Sunday 28 November 2021, new bus Route 816 Keysborough South - Noble Park Station will be introduced to the network to provide public transport coverage to the growing residential area of south Keysborough.

  • Route 816 will stop at key destinations including Parkmore Shopping Centre, Sirius College Keysborough Campus, Keysborough Gardens Primary school, Keysborough South Shopping Centre, and Mt Hira college.
  • Route 816 will operate every 30 minutes during the weekday peak and every 60 minutes off peak and on weekends.
  • Services will run between 6am to 9pm on weekdays, 7am to 9pm on Saturdays and 8am to 9pm on Sundays.
  • Services will be coordinated with trains during peak periods at Noble Park Station.
  • On school days, Route 816 will deviate via Perry Road and Pacific Drive to service Mount Hira College and coordinate with morning and afternoon school bell times.

Route 816 Keysborough South to Noble Park Station departs from Perry Road and travels via Hutton Road, Chapel Road, Church Road, Perry Road, Kingsclere Avenue, Parkmore Shopping Centre, Kingsclere Avenue, Bundeena Avenue, Wahroonga Avenue, Buckley Street, Noble Street, Leonard Avenue, terminating at Noble Park Station.

On return, Route 816 departs Noble Park Station and travels via Mons Parade, Heatherton Road, Douglas Street, Franks Street, Noble Street, Buckley Street, Wahroonga Avenue, Bundeena Avenue, Kingsclere Avenue, Parkmore Shopping Centre, Kingsclere Avenue, Perry Road, Church Road, Chapel Road, Hutton Road, terminating at Perry Road.

Route 813 Dandenong - Waverley Gardens Shopping Centre

  • Route 813 will have a new timetable with extended operating hours and improved frequency.
  • Route 813 weekday services will run every 40 minutes and weekend services will continue to operate hourly.
  • Route 813 will be coordinated with trains at Dandenong Station during peak periods.
  • Sunday services will commence earlier with the last trips extended slightly on all days of the week.
  • The route alignment will remain unchanged.

Routes 815 Dandenong - Noble Park

  • With the introduction of Route 816 and service upgrades to Route 813, Route 815 will be discontinued and replaced by sections of routes 813 and 816.
  • Route 815 passengers are encouraged to check the timetables of routes 816 and 813 to understand what alternative travel options are available to them.
