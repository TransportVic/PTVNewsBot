Boost Mobile AUSX Open Supercross

The world’s best Supercross athletes’ storm into Melbourne for the most electrifying night of supercross action. Marvel Stadium becomes the battleground as Australia’s biggest Supercross event, the AUSX Open, returns for high-speed, bar-to-bar chaos. Expect international talent and non-stop entertainment.

Event commences: 6:00pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey. 

If you use Journey Planner within 14 days of this event, you'll see all the extra services we've added to help you get there.
