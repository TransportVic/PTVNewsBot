New tram timetables on routes 86 and 96

The change will improve reliability, journey time accuracy and better align with how people travel.

On weekends, routes 86 and 96 will run more often at busy times. It will be easier to get where you’re going, with less waiting and less crowding.

Journey times on some trips will be up to five minutes longer to reflect current travel conditions, making travel times more accurate.

To plan your journey:

  • Download the PTV app
  • Use the journey planner
  • Check the new Route 86 and Route 96 timetables
  • or call 1800 800 007.
