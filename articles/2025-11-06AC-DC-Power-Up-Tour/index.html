AC/DC Power Up Tour

The world's greatest rock'n'roll band returns to Australia for the first time in a decade!

The concert will begin at 8:00pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey Planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey. 

Wednesday 12 November

Metropolitan train

Additional services for the following lines arrive prior to the concert:

  • Mernda, Greensborough, Glen Waverley, Sandringham, Craigieburn and Sunbury passengers.

Additional services for the following lines depart after the concert:

  • Mernda, Epping, Greensborough, MacLeod, Flinders Steet, Ringwood, Glen Waverley, Caulfield, Frankston, Sandringham, Newport, Craigieburn and Sunbury passengers.

East Pakenham & Cranbourne line passengers:

  • Board Caulfield-bound services at Richmond (Platform 6).  
  • Change at Caulfield to the connecting bus to Dandenong.

Werribee line passengers:

  • Board Newport-bound services at Flinders Street.  
  • Change at Newport to the connecting bus.

Staff will be available at stations to assist passengers.
