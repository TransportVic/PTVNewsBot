New bus Route 525 Craigieburn Station to Donnybrook Station

The new bus Route 525 Craigieburn Station to Donnybrook Station will connect you to schools, shops and local services, including:

  • Craigieburn Plaza Shopping Centre
  • Craigieburn Central Shopping Centre
  • Splash Aqua Park and Leisure Centre
  • Mt Ridley College
  • Highlands Shopping Centre

Services start on Sunday 22 December and will run seven days a week. Bus services are coordinated with train services from Craigieburn Station travelling to the city in the mornings, and from the city from 3pm until the last service.

For those departing Donnybrook Station, please note that the bus will first travel to Olivine Boulevard to safely turn around before heading to Kalkallo, Mickleham and Craigieburn.

This is a temporary arrangement until Donnybrook Station bus interchange opens in 2020. There are no bus stops along Olivine Boulevard or opposite Donnybrook Station, so passengers will not be able to get on or off there.
