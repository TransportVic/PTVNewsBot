Public transport for the A-League Round 29 – Melbourne Victory v Newcastle Jets

Events > Sport

Join Melbourne Victory for the final game of the home and away season as they take on the Newcastle Jets at AAMI Park. Public transport is your best way to enjoy the action.

We're running extra services to help you get home from AAMI Park on Sunday 4 May.

Kick-off is at 5pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Please note the following works which may impact your journey:

  • Passengers on the Werribee line, please note, buses replace trains between Newport and Werribee from 9pm.
  • Passengers on the Frankston line, please board a Pakenham service at Richmond, and transfer at Caulfield for a connecting train.
