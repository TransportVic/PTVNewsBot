Public transport for Flemington Finals Race Day: Saturday 5 July 2025

Events > Racing

Horse Racing returns to Melbourne at the Flemington Racecourse! Public transport is your best way to enjoy it, with extra services to help you get to and from the Flemington Racecourse on Saturday 5 July 2025.

  • Gates Open: 11.15am
  • First Race: 11.55am
  • Main Race: 3.30pm
  • Last Race: 4.40pm

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Please visit the event website for further event information here.

 Metropolitan train

Getting to Flemington Racecourse

Trains will operate from Southern Cross (Platform 8) stopping only at North Melbourne (Platform 2), enroute to Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1).

  • From 11am, services will depart every 30 minutes.

During the above period, services will return to Southern Cross via North Melbourne with passengers.

The last departure from Southern Cross will be at 1.30pm

Leaving from Flemington Racecourse

Trains will operate from Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1), stopping only at North Melbourne (Platform 1) enroute to Southern Cross (Platform 8).

Trains will depart Flemington Racecourse approximately as follows:

  • 20 minutes after Race 7: 3.50pm
  • 20 minutes after Race 8: 4.25pm
  • 20 minutes after Last Race: 5pm
  • Then depart at: 5.20pm, 5.40pm, 6pm

During the above period, services will operate to Flemington Racecourse via North Melbourne with passengers.

The final service from Flemington Racecourse will depart at 6pm from (Platform 1).

 Metropolitan tram

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

Route 57 

  • Trams will operate via Route 57 from Stop 01 Flinders Street / Elizabeth Street and passengers can alight at Stop 32 Sandown Road/ Epsom Road via Flemington Road.

 Metropolitan bus

Getting to and from Flemington Racecourse

The following bus route travels close to Flemington Racecourse: 

  • Route 472 (Williamstown to Moonee Ponds via Footscray)

Passengers can catch buses from Footscray Station and alight at Langs Road/Showgrounds.

Getting to Flemington Racecourse

Passengers arriving at North Melbourne and Southern Cross stations are advised to change trains and catch a Flemington Racecourse service.
