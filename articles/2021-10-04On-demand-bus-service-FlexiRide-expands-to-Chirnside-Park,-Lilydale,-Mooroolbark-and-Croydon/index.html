On-demand bus service FlexiRide expands to Chirnside Park, Lilydale, Mooroolbark and Croydon

From Monday 4 October 2021, new on-demand service FlexiRide will help residents in Chirnside Park, Lilydale, Mooroolbark and Croydon get to work, local shopping centres and transport hubs.

FlexiRide is a 12-month trial and will replace Telebus routes 1,2,3 and 4 and Route 676.

FlexiRide has no fixed route and only operates when booked. Passengers can book a trip from their nearest physical or virtual bus stop and travel to and from the designated “FlexiRide bus hubs” in the same service area.

FlexiRide will operate from 6am to 8pm on weekdays and 8am to 6pm on Saturdays. Services do not run on Sundays or public holidays.

To find your nearest stop location or book FlexiRide, call (03) 8710 6377 between 5am – 7.30pm or download the FlexiRide app from Google Play or Apple App Store. You can book up to seven days in advance. Bookings via the app can be cancelled at any time.

Please let us know if you have accessibility needs when making a booking. Customers with accessibility needs can register for home pickup by calling (03) 8710 6377.

FlexiRide is a cashless service so make sure you top up your myki before you travel.

The off-peak Telebus service on Route 672 Croydon to Chirnside Park will be removed with this change. All services will run the fixed route path along Yarra Road. The timetable will remain unchanged.

For more information about FlexiRide or how to use the service call 1800 800 007.
