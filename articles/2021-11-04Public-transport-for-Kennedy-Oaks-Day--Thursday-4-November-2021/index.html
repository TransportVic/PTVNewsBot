Public transport for Kennedy Oaks Day: Thursday 4 November 2021

Get frocked up for Ladies Day at the 2021 Spring Racing Carnival at Flemington Racecourse!

Additional services will help you get easily to and from the Kennedy Oaks Day on Thursday 4 November 2021.

Before you travel

Please check in every time you see QR codes at stations and stops and while you travel on trains, trams and buses (even if have already touched on with a registered myki).

We strongly encourage you to register your myki so that we can contact you if you have been exposed to coronavirus (COVID-19) on a public transport service. Register your myki now - it’s quick and easy.

When travelling on public transport you must wear a fitted face mask covering your nose and mouth, unless you have a lawful reason for not doing so, and ensure you wear it for your entire journey.

Please check the Disruptions page for notification of any service changes that may affect your journey to and from the event.

Oaks Day - Thursday 4 November 2021

All services will operate to a normal Thursday timetable, with additional services as noted below.

  • Gates open: 10.40am
  • First Race: 12.40pm
  • Feature Race: 5.10pm
  • Last Race: 5.50pm

 Metropolitan train

Getting to the Racecourse

Trains will depart from Flinders Street Station (Platform 8/9) to Flemington Racecourse stopping at Southern Cross Station (Platform 14), North Melbourne (Platform 6) and express to Flemington Racecourse Station.

  • From 10.22am to 1.22pm, every 20 minutes

Leaving the Racecourse

Trains will depart from Flemington Racecourse (Platform 1) to Flinders Street Station (Platforms 8/9), stopping at North Melbourne (Platform 5) and Southern Cross (Platform 13):

  • From 5.30pm to 6.00pm, every 15 minutes
  • From 6.00pm to 6.30pm, every 10 minutes
  • From 6.30pm to 7.51pm, every 20 minutes

Plan your journey using Journey planner.

The last train departs Flemington Racecourse Station at 7.51pm from Platform 1.

Passengers can catch a Route 57 tram, alighting at Stop 29 Racecourse Road/Flemington Racecourse.
