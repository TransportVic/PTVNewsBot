New look Mobile myki is here

Android users will see changes to Mobile myki over the next few months.

From Sunday 1 June, customers will see a new Mobile myki card design. There will also be some minor changes to the settings and how the card looks in the Google WalletTM . Customers can still top up myki Money and myki Pass for full fare and general concession.

For more information and help setting up, visit Mobile myki.
