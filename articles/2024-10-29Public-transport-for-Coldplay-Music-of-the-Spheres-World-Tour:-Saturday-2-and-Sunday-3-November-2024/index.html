Public transport for Coldplay Music of the Spheres World Tour: Saturday 2 and Sunday 3 November 2024

Heading to Coldplay? With extra services to get you there and back, public transport is the way to go. Take advantage of the city’s Free Tram Zone which extends from Queen Victoria Market to Docklands, Spring Street,

Flinders Street Station and Federation Square to get to Coldplay’s record-breaking Music of The Spheres World Tour at Marvel Stadium on Saturday 2 and Sunday 3 November.

Gates open at 5.00pm.

  • Saturday 2 November
  • Sunday 3 November
