Minor timetable adjustments for Warrnambool V/Line coaches

From Sunday 11 July 2021, minor timetable adjustments are being made to better reflect journey times on select V/Line coach services from Warrnambool. Passengers at some stops along each route will have slightly earlier departure times.

Monday to Friday: 10.59pm Warrnambool – Portland
The service will maintain the current 10.59pm departure time from Warrnambool and 12.24am arrival time at Portland. Passengers at Port Fairy, Yambuk, Tyrendarra and Narrawong will have slightly earlier departure times.

Saturday: 10.37pm Warrnambool – Heywood
The service will maintain the current 10.37pm departure time from Warrnambool and 12.22am arrival time at Heywood.

Passengers at Port Fairy, Yambuk, Tyrendarra, Narrawong and Portland will have slightly earlier departure times.

Sunday: 10.37pm Warrnambool – Mount Gambier
The service will maintain the current 10.37pm departure time from Warrnambool and will arrive one minute earlier for a 12.51am arrival time at Mount Gambier. Passengers at Portland will have a slightly earlier departure time.

To plan your journey, visit the Journey planner or order a printed copy of a timetable by calling 1800 800 007.
