Public transport for Round 1 of the 2024 AFL season

Public transport is the best way to get to the game during Round 1 of the 2024 AFL season!

Extra services will run to and from the MCG, Marvel Stadium and GMHBA Stadium.

Plan your trip to the game using Journey planner.

Collingwood v Sydney , MCG on Friday 15 March 2024 at 7:40pm

Essendon v Hawthorn , MCG on Saturday 16 March 2024 at 1:45pm

Geelong v St Kilda, GMHBA Stadium on Saturday 16 March 2024 at 7:30pm

Melbourne v Western Bulldogs , MCG on 17 March 2024 at 1pm

Before you travel

We strongly encourage you to register your myki. With a registered myki you can protect your myki balance if the card is lost or stolen, check your travel history and manage multiple myki cards in the one account.

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.
