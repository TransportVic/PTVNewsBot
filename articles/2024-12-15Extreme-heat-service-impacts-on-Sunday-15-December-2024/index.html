Extreme heat service impacts on Sunday 15 December 2024

Travelling in Victoria > Regional Victoria

Hot weather is forecast for parts of Victoria on Sunday 15 December 2024.

Extreme heat timetables will be operating across parts of the V/Line network. This means journey times may be extended by a few minutes, and some services will be replaced by air-conditioned coaches for all or part of the journey.
