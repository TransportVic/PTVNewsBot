Public transport for the NRL Round 17 –  Melbourne Storm v Cronulla Sharks:  Sunday 29 June 2025Extra services will help you get home from AAMI Park.

Kick-off is at 2:00 pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Disruptions:

  • For passengers on the Frankston line, buses replace trains between Cheltenham and Frankston.
  • For passengers on the Stony Point line, buses replace trains between Frankston and Stony Point.
  • For passengers on the Sunbury line, buses replace trains between North Melbourne and Sunbury, and between Watergardens and Sunbury.
