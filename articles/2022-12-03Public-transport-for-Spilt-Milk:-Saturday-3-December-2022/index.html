Public transport for Spilt Milk: Saturday 3 December 2022

Travelling to Ballarat for Spilt Milk on Saturday 3rd December? Passengers can travel via a mixture of regular and additional VLine train services to Ballarat, then transfer to the Spilt Milk Bus Shuttle from Ballarat Station direct to Victoria Park.

Remember to top up your myki before the event and to wear a fitted face mask for your entire journey.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner and check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

Getting to and from Ballarat

 Regional train(V/Line)

TO BALLARAT

 Regular Services

Passengers travelling to Ballarat can board regular train services between Melbourne Southern Cross and Ballarat, running every 60 minutes from 7:14am.

Additional Services

Passengers travelling to Ballarat from Melbourne can travel via the following Additional Train Services:

  • VLine Service travelling from Southern Cross and stopping all stations to Ballarat, departing 8:45am and arriving 10:12am at Ballarat.

TO MELBOURNE

 Additional Services

Passengers returning to Melbourne from Ballarat can travel via the following Additional Train Services:

  • VLine Services travelling from Ballarat and stopping all stations to Southern Cross, departing 10:39pm and arriving 12:07am at Southern Cross.
  • VLine Services travelling from Ballarat and stopping all stations to Southern Cross , departing 12:01am and arriving 1:29am at Southern Cross. 

Regular Services

 Passengers returning to Melbourne via Regular Services can board the following regular services:

  • Depart Ballarat at 6:20pm, arrive at 7:48pm at Southern Cross
  • Depart Ballarat at 7:20pm, arrive at 8:48pm at Southern Cross

Depart Ballarat at 9:28pm, arrive at 10:56pm at Southern Cross

 Regional Bus

There are regular bus services running via Gillies Street on Route 26 Alfredton - Ballarat Station until 8pm, passengers can board and alight at the following bus stops and walk to the main entrance on Gillies Street:

To Alfredton

  • Elm Ave/Gillies St S (Alfredton)
  • Abraham Pl/Leopold St (Alfredton)

To Ballarat Station

  • Abraham Pl/Leopold St (Alfredton) 

Spilt Milk Bus Shuttle

Passengers can board a dedicated return bus shuttle from Mair Street (at Coffee Palace Lane) to Victoria Park (Gillies Street). Return services will also travel via Sturt Street and Mair Street.

Please visit the Spilt Milk website for further information and updates on the Spilt Milk Bus Shuttle schedule.
