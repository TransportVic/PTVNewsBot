New timetables for Routes 400, 410, 420 and 422

From Monday 29 January, we’re introducing new timetables to bus routes Route 400 Sunshine Station - Laverton Station via Robinsons Road, Route 410 Sunshine Station - Footscray via Ballarat Road, 420 Sunshine Station - Watergardens Station via Deer Park and Route 422 Sunshine Station - Brimbank Central SC via Deer Park.

The changes will make it easier for locals to get where they need to go, with better connections between buses and trains.

In addition to the timetable changes, from Tuesday 30 January, the Caroline Springs – Deer Park V/Line shuttle service will no longer run.

Passengers should check timetables before they travel.

New timetables are available at ptv.vic.gov.au, the PTV app or by calling 1800 800 007.
