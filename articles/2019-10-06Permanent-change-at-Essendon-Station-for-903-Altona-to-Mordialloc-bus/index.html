Permanent change at Essendon Station for 903 Altona to Mordialloc bus

From Sunday 6 October 2019, a temporary bus route alignment will become permanent for Route 903 Altona to Mordialloc.

The minor route adjustment is a safer path to travel following the installation of traffic lights at the intersection of Mt Alexander Road into Shamrock Street.

Services to Altona will travel southbound via Rose Street and stop at the entrance of Essendon Station.

Services to Mordialloc will travel north and stop at Mt Alexander Road. This stop provides the bus driver and passengers a safer view of traffic and will help minimise traffic delays.

Minor timetable adjustments will be made to give passengers more reliable information and better reflect actual running times. The new timetable will start from Sunday 6 October.
