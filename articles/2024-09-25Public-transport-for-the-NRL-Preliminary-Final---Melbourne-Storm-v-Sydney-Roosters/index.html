Public transport for the NRL Preliminary Final – Melbourne Storm v Sydney Roosters

NRL Finals are on at AAMI Park! This matchup is sure to deliver edge-of-your-seat action and public transport is your best way to enjoy it, as the Melbourne Storm takes on the Sydney Roosters. Extra services will help you get to and from AAMI Park on Friday 27 September.

The event will start at 7:50pm.

Before you travel

Plan your journey using Journey planner. Check Disruptions for service changes that may affect your journey.

 Metropolitan train

Extra trains will run to get you home from the game.

Return trains from Richmond Station

  • Ringwood leaving at 10:02pm and 10:22pm from Platform 9
  • Glen Waverley leaving at 10:04pm and 10:24pm from Platform 10
  • Dandenong leaving at 10:01pm and 10:25pm from Platform 6
  • Frankston leaving at 10:09pm from Platform 4
  • Cheltenham leaving at 10:18pm from Platform 4
  • Sandringham leaving at 10:14pm from Platform 2

 Metropolitan tram

Extra trams will run before and after the game.

Getting to and from AAMI Park

Extra Route 70 trams will run between Flinders Street / Elizabeth Street (Stop 4) and Stop 7C MCG/John Cain Arena.

 Metropolitan bus

Customers can catch a Route 246 bus close to AAMI Park.

The last bus may run before the event ends.

 Regional train

Catch a Route 70 or 70a tram via Flinders Street or a metropolitan train running to and from Richmond Station to get to AAMI Park.
