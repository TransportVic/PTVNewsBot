New bus interchange at Merinda Park Station

From Sunday 16 June, routes 863 Endeavour Hills SC – Cranbourne West via Hallam Road and 881 Merinda Park Station – Clyde North will have minor route and timetable changes to access the new bus interchange at Merinda Park Station.

Route 863 will make a small diversion from Evans Road via Thompson Road and Merinda Park Boulevard to access the bus interchange. Currently, Route 863 stops on Evans Road near the station.

Route 881 will make a small diversion from Thompson Road via Merinda Park Boulevard to access the bus interchange. Currently, Route 881 stops on Thompson Road and Merinda Park Boulevard. 

The new bus interchange provides a direct and convenient connection between train and bus services at Merinda Park Station.
