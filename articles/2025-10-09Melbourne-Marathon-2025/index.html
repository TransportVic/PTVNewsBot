Melbourne Marathon 2025

The Melbourne Marathon returns to the MCG precinct, starting on Batman Avenue from 6am. 

Public transport services will be running to and from the start line on Batman Avenue, outside the MCG Precinct on Sunday 12 October 2025.

Passengers, please note there will be service changes for trams and buses from 4am to 2pm due to the road closures for the Melbourne Marathon. 

Travelling via car? Please note there will be road closures from 4am which may impact on your journey. See here for further information.
