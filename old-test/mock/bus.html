Service changes

Permanent changes to buses including new routes, timetable upgrades and route changes. For more information about any of these changes, call 1800 800 007 or follow the links below.

New bus interchange at Merinda Park Station

From Sunday 16 June 2024, routes 863 Endeavour Hills SC – Cranbourne West via Hallam Road and 881 Merinda Park Station – Clyde North will have minor route and timetable changes to access the new bus interchange at Merinda Park Station.

New bus network for Torquay and Armstrong Creek 

From 2 June 2024, there will be changes to the Geelong, Charlemont, Armstrong Creek, Torquay and Jan Juc bus network. 

Timetable improvements for Route 606

From Sunday 19 May 2024, Route 606 Elsternwick Station – Fishermans Bend will have an improved timetable with more services, longer operating hours and better connections with trains.

New bus route for Sunbury and Diggers Rest

Starting 24 March, the new bus service, Route 475 Sunbury Station – Diggers Rest Station, will deliver better connections to shops, schools, train stations and other amenities.

Have your say about proposed changes to the Gisborne bus network

We’re improving the Gisborne bus network and want to hear your feedback on the proposed changes. We’re proposing changes to improve efficiency and meet passenger demands to accommodate a growing Gisborne. Your feedback will help shape our plans for better bus services.

New services for Albury to Seymour coach

From Saturday 24 February, there will be new coach services between Seymour and Albury, giving passengers more options for travelling to and from Melbourne.

Hampton bus interchange relocated

From Sunday 4 February, the new Hampton bus interchange will open on Koolkuna Lane, as part of the Hampton Station Precinct development.

New timetables for Routes 400, 410, 420 and 422

From Monday 29 January, we’re introducing new timetables for bus routes Route 400 Sunshine Station - Laverton Station via Robinsons Road, Route 410 Sunshine Station - Footscray via Ballarat Road, 420 Sunshine Station - Watergardens Station via Deer Park and Route 422 Sunshine Station - Brimbank Central SC via Deer Park.

New bus service to connect Donnybrook and Craigieburn stations

From 7 January 2024, Route 501 Donnybrook Station – Craigieburn Station via Hume Fwy will provide a fast and frequent connection between regional and metro train services. There will also be a timetable change to Route 511 Donnybrook Station – Mandalay via Olivine Estate which will come into effect on the same day.

Better bus and train connections in Craigieburn and the wider Hume area

From 7 January 2024, Route 390 Mernda Station – Craigieburn Station via Wollert will start earlier and finish later, providing more services and improved connections between trains and buses.

Faster and more direct travel along Camp Road in Broadmeadows

From 17 December 2023, passengers on Route 538 will travel direct along Camp Road, making for a more reliable and faster journey.

Upgraded service for Route 631 and removal of Route 821

From 3 December 2023, an upgraded service for Route 631 and the removal of Route 821 will provide a simpler bus network with reduced duplication and an enhanced frequency and span of hours for travellers. 

New bus interchange on the south side of Tarneit Railway Station

From 19 November 2023, 8 bus routes that services the southern side of Tarneit will be relocated from the existing northern interchange to the new southern side interchange.

Upcoming improvements to the Yarra Valley bus network

From 26 November 2023, changes will be introduced to the Yarra Valley bus network for routes 684, 685, 686 and 687.

Extra bus services to Chadstone Shopping Centre connecting to trains and trams

Two additional bus services will operate to get to Chadstone Shopping Centre.

More bus services over more hours for Fishermans Bend

From 15 October 2023, Fishermans Bend residents, workers and visitors are set to get a public transport boost with more bus services running more often, over more of the day.

Improved services for Box Hill, Burwood and Deakin University bus routes

From 15 October 2023, more buses more often will be available for Box Hill, Burwood and Deakin University passengers.

Better buses for Casey

From Sunday 26 June 2023, improvements will be introduced on bus routes 863 and 895 in the City of Casey.

New bus timetables from Sunday 28 May 2023

From Sunday 28 May 2023, bus routes 343, 356, 357, 358, 381, 382, 383, 384, 385, 386, 387, 388, 389, 564, 569, 577, 578, 579, 580 and 582 will have new timetables.

Improvements to the Yarra Valley bus network

Following community consultation, the Department of Transport and Planning is making improvements to the Yarra Valley bus network.

Changes to Kilmore Town Service (Link Bus)

From Monday 24 April 2023, the 11:37am weekday service from Kilmore East Station to Kilmore will change to depart 7 minutes later.

More services for routes 733 and 767

From 30 April 2023, more services will be introduced on routes 733 and 767.

Changes to bus routes 200, 305, 309 and 905

Bendigo – Shepparton via Kyabram timetable change

From Sunday 29 January 2023, the Bendigo – Shepparton via Kyabram route will have a new timetable.

Colac Route 3 timetable and minor route change

Route 505 extra weekday trips

Changes to Berwick Station Bus Interchange

From Sunday 11 December 2022, there will be permanent access changes to Berwick Station Bus Interchange.

Improved bus links between Alexandra and Eildon

From Monday 21 November 2022, the Alexandra to Eildon bus service will be boosted to operate every weekday.

Bus service to connect Smythesdale, Smythes Creek, Haddon and Snake Valley to Delacombe Town Centre

Minor bus timetable changes for Wallan

New coach timetable along the Shepparton Line

Let's Go with more buses between the city and Fishermans Bend

From Monday 31 October 2022, there will be more buses to take you from the city to Fishermans Bend.

Bus changes are coming to Clyde and Clyde North

Timetable and route path changes for Glenrowan, Wangaratta and Yarrawonga bus services

More frequent buses on Route 390

From Sunday 7 August, Route 390 Mernda Station – Craigieburn Station via Wollert buses will run more frequently helping residents get to work, school and connect with other transport services.

Minor route and timetable change for bus Route 15

From 3 July 2022, buses between Ballarat Station – Brown Hill will travel via Fussell and Charlesworth Street.

Changes to the Wyndham bus network

From 26 June 2022, there will be minor timetable and route path changes on bus services across the City of Wyndham. This is due to a V/Line rail timetable change and the new bus interchange at Hoppers Crossing Station.

Bus and coach timetable adjustments for improved travel on the Geelong and Warrnambool lines

From Sunday 29 May 2022, the timetable for a number of bus and coach services will change to maintain reliable connections with the adjusted train timetable along the Geelong and Warrnambool lines.

New Route 881 bus service to connect Merinda Park Station and Clyde North
