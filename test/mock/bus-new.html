Service changes

Permanent changes to buses including new routes, timetable upgrades and route changes. For more information about any of these changes, call 1800 800 007 or follow the links below.

We’re adding 470 weekly trips across routes 170, 180, 190 and 192

From 1 July 2025, we’re adding more buses, more often on routes 170, 180, 190 and 192.

Read more

Route 798 extended to Clyde North

From 11 May 2025, Route 798 will extend from Cranbourne to Clyde North.

Read more

Wangaratta to Albury coach extended for Benalla passengers 

From Monday 31 March 2025, the 7:30am weekday Wangaratta to Albury coach will depart from Benalla at 7am.

Read more

Bus and coach timetable changes to better connect with regional trains

From 13 April 2025, there will be new bus and coach timetables to better connect with new train timetables on the Ballarat and Warrnambool lines.

Read more

Route 925 extended to Officer South

From 23 March 2025, Route 925 will extend from Lakeside in Pakenham to Officer South, connecting with Cardinia Road Station and Kaduna Park.

Read more

Routes 357 and 525 timetable changes

From 14 February 2025, there will be minor changes to three Route 357 Night Buses from Epping Station. From 17 February 2025, two Route 525 morning buses from Craigieburn Station will also have changes.  

Read more

New bus service for Donnybrook

From 16 March 2025, we’re introducing a new bus service in Donnybrook, Olivine and Peppercorn Hill. Route 524 will make it easier for residents to access the train station and schools.

Read more

New bus service for Lucas in Ballarat

From Sunday 23 February 2025, Route 10 will extend to Lucas, providing the growing area with public transport and direct connections to Ballarat and Wendouree Stations.

Read more

New bus interchange for Greensborough Station

A new bus interchange has opened at Para Road and Flintoff Street, providing a safe and easy way to connect between trains and buses at Greensborough Station.

Read more

Route 903 timetable changes

From Sunday 19 January 2025, Route 903 will have a new timetable to provide a more reliable journey for passengers.

Read more

Doncaster Park and Ride timetable changes

From 12 January 2025, there will be timetable changes to 12 buses that travel through Doncaster Park and Ride. Routes 200, 318, 905 and 906 will also have timetable changes. 

Read more

Sunbury Station Bus Interchange and Car Park Projects
